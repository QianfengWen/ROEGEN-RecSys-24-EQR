'''Jiamusi''' (佳木斯; Jiāmùsī) is a city in [[Heilongjiang]] Province in [[China]].

==Understand==
Jiamusi is located on the riverside of Songhua River and has a population of 1.8 million.  The city consists of the following districts
*'''Qianjin District''' (前进区; Qiánjìnqū) 
*'''Xiangyang District''' (向阳区; Xiàngyángqū) 
*'''Dongfeng District''' (东风区; Dōngfēngqū) 
*'''Jiaoqu District''' (郊区; Jiāoqū) 
*'''Huanan District''' (桦南区; Huànánqū) 
*'''Tangyuan County''' (汤原县; Tāngyuánxiàn) 
*'''Tongjiang City''' (同江市; Tóngjiāngshì) 
*'''Fujin City''' (富锦市; Fùjǐnshì) 
*'''Fuyuan County''' (抚远县; Fǔyuǎnxiàn) 
*'''Huachuan County''' (桦川县; Huàchuānxiàn) 

==Get in==

===By plane===
There are flights from Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenyang and Harbin, and international flights from Russia.  The airport (JMU) is 10km east of the city centre. Taxis from the airport may try to charge up to 100 RMB for the 15 minute ride, 30 RMB is more reasonable.

===By train===
There are frequent trains from Harbin, which take 6-8 hours and cost &yen;36-196.  Other trains include departures from Shenyang (13-15 hours; &yen;67-362), Tianjin (23-24 hours; &yen;179-544), Beijing (24 hours; &yen;179-544), Dalian (20 hours), Yantai (36 hours; &yen;248-750), Panjin (16-20 hours; &yen;78-411), Hegang, Mudanjiang and Shuangyashan.

===By bus===
The main bus station is at 146 Zhanqian Lu, Qianjin District.

===By boat===
There are boats along the river from Harbin in the summer months.
