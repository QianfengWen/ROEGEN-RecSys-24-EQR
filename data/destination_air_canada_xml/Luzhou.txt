'''Luzhou District (Chinese: 蘆洲區)''' also known as Lujhou District is a residential district of [[New Taipei City]].

It is the second smallest district in New Taipei City and the third most populated district in Taiwan, after Yonghe District in New Taipei and Da'an District in Taipei. Attractions of this district are Li Family Abode and Luzhou Night Market.
