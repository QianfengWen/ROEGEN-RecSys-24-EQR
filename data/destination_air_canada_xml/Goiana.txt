'''Goiana''' is a city in [[Pernambuco]], [[Brazil]].  Historic buildings, beautiful beaches and original cultural manifestations are the main touristic attractions.

==Get in==

Fly into [[Recife]]'s Guararapes International Airport.  

From Recife, take [[BR-101]] north.  Goiana is 78 Km north of Recife.

Bus Station: Avenida Marechal Castelo Branco, tel.: (81)3626-0130.

==Get around==

==See==

===Historic sites===

* Our Lady of Carmel Church and Convent of Saint Albert of the Carmelites (Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Carmo e Convento de Santo Alberto das Carmelitas), [http://fradescarmelitas.vilabol.uol.com.br/goiana/convento.html].  Built in 1666.
* Our Lady of Blackmen Rosary Church (Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Rosário dos Pretos).  Built in 1596.  Houses a Museum of Sacred Art.
* Our Lady of Whitemen Rosary Church (Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Rosário dos Brancos).  Built in 1705.
* Tejucupapo. Site of the 1646 Tejucupapo Battle, in which local women defeated Dutch troops.  An obelisk (built in 1931) celebrates the victory of the Tejucupapo Heroines.
* Our Lady of Ó Church (Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Ó). Built in 1864, in Ponta de Pedras beach.
* Saint Anthony Church (Igreja de Santo Antônio).  Built in 1887, in Catuama beach.
* Saint Lawrence Church (Igreaja de São Lourenço), [http://www.cultura.pe.gov.br/patrimonio12_tejucupapo.html].  Built in the 16th century.

* Ponta de Pedras beach
* Carne de Vaca beach
* Catuama beach
* Barra de Catuama beach

==Do==

* Catamaran Boat Rides.  Goiana river, Celeiro Island, Itamaracá Island.
* Aparauá EcoAventura [http://www.aparaua.com.br/].  Environment and adventure sports (trekking, biking, mountain biking, rapel).
* Scuba Diving: Wrecks of the Nicolau Copérnico (1883) and the Vapor Bahia (1887) at Ponta de Pedras beach.
* Watch trained crabs offer you drinks at Buraco da Gia restaurant.
