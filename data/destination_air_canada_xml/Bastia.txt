'''Bastia''' is a city on the [[France|France]] island of [[Corsica]].

==Get in==
From Bastia Airport, a bus runs almost every hour for €9 - timetable at the tourist information desk at the airport.

==Get around==
===By boat===
There are several national and international ferry routes from Bastia, to 
[[Ajaccio]] ([[Corsica]]),
[[Genoa]] ([[Italy]]),
[[Marseille]],
[[Nice]],
[[Livorno]] ([[Italy]]) and
[[Savona]].
It is very busy during the summer, and it is always best to book early.

==See==
'''The Vieux Port''' ('Old Port') is a wonderful part of the city. Tall, faded buildings surround a marina with posh yachts. There's certainly something of the 'shabby chic' about the place.
