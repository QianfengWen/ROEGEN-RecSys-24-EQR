'''Karamay''' (克拉玛依; ''Kèlāmǎyī'') is a city in [[Xinjiang]] Province in [[China]]. It is famous as an oil base for drilling in the surrounding desert.  The oil industry contributes to the city's wealth which is evident in the clean and spacious streets.
==Get in==
Buses and taxis leave for Urumqi regularly.  The trip is approximately three hours by taxi and four by bus.  Taxis are about &yen;100 per person.  There are also flights to and from Urumqi.
