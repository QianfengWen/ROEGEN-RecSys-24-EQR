[http://www.whangareinz.org.nz/ '''Whangarei'''] (M&#0257;ori:''Whang&#0257;rei'' - pronounced: ''Faa - ngaa - re - i'') is the major population centre of [[Northland]] and the northernmost "city" in the [[North Island]] of [[New Zealand]] with about 50,000 residents.  It has a warm semi-tropical climate, with summer daytime temperatures around 24-26Â°C, regular rainy days year round and mild winters that rarely have frosts and are mild enough for the region to be described as ''the winterless north''.

==Get in==

===By bus===
'''InterCity''' [http://www.intercitycoach.co.nz] runs buses from Auckland to Kataia with a stop in Whangarei.

===By car===
Whangarei is around two hours drive north of [[Auckland]]. One hour south of the [[Bay of Islands]] and one hour east of Dargaville.

===By plane===
Whangarei Airport (WRE) is about ten minutes drive from the central city in the suburb of Onerahi. Taxis and a shuttle service as well as car rentals are available from the airport to town.<br> 
*'''Air New Zealand''' [http://www.airnz.co.nz] has several daily flights from Auckland. Flying time is 30 to 40 minutes.

==Get around==

===By bus===
Whangarei's public transport is [http://www.wdc.govt.nz/customerservice/?lc=reader&m=tssd&i=2992] run by Citylink the Whangarei District and Regional councils. It covers most of the suburbs and the inner city. Tickets can be purchased from the driver. Look for the Green Busses and timetables are located at bus stops.

==Get out==
* [[Poor_Knights_Islands#See|Poor Knights Islands]] &mdash; Dive and Snorkle by Charter or by private boat. Note: Can not land on the islands unless with a permit
Whangarei is 70 kilometres from the [[Bay of Islands]] and is the primary gateway to the rest of [[Northland]]. Alternatively you can take the road to Dargaville and travel north from there through the Kauri forests and along the rugged west coast beaches to Opononi and the Hokianga.
* '''Do the [[Hokianga]]''' (90 minutes drive from Whangarei)
** '''[[Horeke#See|Mangungu Mission House]]''' &mdash; site of the biggest signing of the Treaty of Waitangi
** '''[[Koutu]]''' &mdash; biggest concretions in the world
** '''[[Opononi]]''' &mdash; fantastic sandstone sculptures on top of dune opposite Opononi
** '''[[Horeke#See|Wairere Boulders (''Nature Park'')]]''' &mdash; an enormous basalt flow just south of Horeke
** '''[[Hokianga#See|Omahuta Kauri Sanctuary]]''' &mdash; see 5 of the 12 biggest Kauri trees in an undisturbed healthy forest
