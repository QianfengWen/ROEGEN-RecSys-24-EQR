'''Ndola''' is in the Copperbelt in [[Zambia]]

==Get in==
The largest town in the copperbelt, '''Ndola''' has an airstrip serviced by Air Zambia from [[Lusaka]]. Ndola is about a 4 to 5 hour drive from Lusaka on a well paved road. Buses run from Lusaka to Ndola. The most reliable company is Mazhandu Family Bus Services [[http://travelmazhandu.com]] which runs several buses a day for 65,000 kwacha (about $13 USD).
