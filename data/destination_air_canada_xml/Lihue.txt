'''Lihue''' [http://www.kauai-hawaii.com/areas.php?areas_id=3], on the island of [[Kauai]] is the island's business and transportation center, home to popular tourist sites such as the Kauai Museum and Menehune Fish Ponds.

==Get in==
===By plane===

The Lihue Airport is the main aviation airport on Kauai
