
Front Royal is a town of 15,000 people (2019) in the  US state of  Virginia's Shenandoah Valley. It is the county seat of Warren County.


Get in[edit]
Front Royal Main Street
The town is at the intersection of Interstate 66, US-340 and US-522 near US-55.  It is just miles from the intersection of Interstate 66 and Interstate 81
The closest airport is 38.262488-78.9002251 Shenandoah Valley Regional Airport (SHD), a small airport with scheduled commercial service only to Chicago–O'Hare and Washington–Dulles.
About an hour drive from Front Royal is 38.956813-77.4464862 Washington Dulles International Airport major airport with numerous domestic and international flights.
For passenger train service, 38.750069-77.472793 Manassas Amtrak Station is about an hour drive from Front Royal.
Passenger bus service is available from 38.897157-77.0063194 Washington Greyhound Bus Station, about a 90-minute drive from Front Royal.
Get around[edit]
Front Royal's downtown area along Main Street is walkable. 
There is a bus, the called Front Royal Trolley, which travels around the town of Front Royal but not the surrounding areas. ($0.50 fare; free for ages 0-12). (Route map)
Otherwise, you will definitely need a car to get around.

38.878141-78.1507481 Appalachian Trail. The 2,100-mile (3,400-km) Appalachian Trail passes near Front Royal. You can find it on Route 522 (Remount Road), about 4 miles southeast of the center of town. From here, you can walk 969 miles (1,560 km) south to Georgia or 1,134 miles (1,825 km) north to Maine. There is a Front Royal Trolley stop here, but pickup is only twice per day.
