
Pendine (Welsh: Pentywyn) is a village in Carmarthenshire.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
3 miles south of the A477.
Bus 222 from Carmarthen and  351 from Tenby.
Closest rail stations are Whitland and Saundersfoot.
