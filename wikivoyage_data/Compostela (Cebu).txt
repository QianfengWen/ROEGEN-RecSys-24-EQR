
Compostela is a municipality in Metro Cebu, on the coast north of Cebu City.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Is about 30 km (19 mi) north of Cebu City on the North Nautical Highway.

By bus[edit]
Many buses to/from Mandaue Cebu North Terminal and to/from Bogo using the "Hagnaya", "Maya" or "Malapascua" bus routes.
