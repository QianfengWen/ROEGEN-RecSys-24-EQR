


Not to be confused with Alton, a village in Staffordshire near the Alton Towers theme park.

Jane Austen's house in nearby Chawton
Alton is a town in Hampshire, surrounded by attractive countryside and villages that were once home to novelist Jane Austen, naturalist Gilbert White, and Antarctic explorer Lawrence Oates.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
It is just off the A31 between Farnham and Winchester.

By train[edit]
Watercress Line, a preserved railway, runs from Alresford to Alton.
The station provides service from London Waterloo, direct or with a change in Guildford or Woking.

The 17th-century house in which novelist Jane Austen spent the last eight years of her life, writing and revising six of her novels.
