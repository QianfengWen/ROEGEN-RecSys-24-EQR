
Ibafo is a town in Ogun State.


Understand[edit]
Historically, the entire town of Ibafo served as a farmland for residents of Igbehin in Abeokuta, Ogun State's capital, who would leave their homes and walk down track ways to Ibafo. They build temporary tents to live in for two to four months while they are there.

Get in[edit]
Being located on the Lagos-Ibadan Express way, Ibafo is easily accessible to Lagos and Ibadan. Most people come in through danfo from Lagos and commercial vehicles on the major road. The predominant means of transportation within Ibafo is motorcycle which cost as low as ₦100 for minimum distance

Get around[edit]
Map of Ibafo
By shared taxi[edit]
Shuttles (Danfo) are abundant in Ibafo, they are hailed down  by the roadside. The driver usually takes 14 passengers three at the back rows and two in front. Fare ₦100-200.

By minibus[edit]
Minibuss are scattered around the city and township plying every nook and cranny  some of the drivers could be erratic. Fare ₦50-100

By tricycle[edit]
Happy go lucky drivers move people around in this. Fare ₦50-100

By motorcycle[edit]
If you desire speed to move around, motorcycles available for a token fare.
