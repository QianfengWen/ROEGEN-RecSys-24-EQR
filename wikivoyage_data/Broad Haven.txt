
Broad Haven (Welsh: Aberllydan) is a seaside village with a safe, sandy Blue Flag beach on the west coast of Pembrokeshire in west Wales. It is located within the Pembrokeshire Coast National Park and on the 186 mile Pembrokeshire Coast Path the and 870 mile Wales Coast Path. It is a base for walkers, wind surfers, all water sports, bird watching and geology.


Understand[edit]
Broad Haven and Little Haven together form the Havens community.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
From Haverfordwest 6 miles along the B4341, or 6.5 miles from Milford Haven via Herbrandston.

By Bus[edit]
Broad Haven is accessible by bus (route 311) from Haverfordwest. A group of coastal buses operate along the Pembrokeshire Coast Path. These enable walkers to join and leave at various points along the 186 miles of the path and return to their starting point.
