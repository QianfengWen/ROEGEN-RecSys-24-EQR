
Roma is a town in the Darling Downs region of Queensland, approximately 480 km west of Brisbane along the Warrego Highway.


Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
Qantaslink fly daily to Roma.

By car[edit]
The Warrego Highway heads west from Brisbane through the Lockyer Valley, up the range to Toowoomba, through the rich pastoral lands of the Darling Downs onwards to Roma. From Roma your journey can continue west to Charleville, north to Emerald and Mackay or south into New South Wales.

By train[edit]
Queensland Rail runs its Westlander service between Brisbane and Charleville two times a week. -26.576103148.7922961 Roma railway station is located at 41 Station St.
