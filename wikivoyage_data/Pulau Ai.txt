Pulau Ai is an island that is part of the Banda Islands.


Understand[edit]
Map of Pulau Ai
The island is limited to a single village with nutmeg and kenari almond plantations. This is a very relaxing place. Ai Island is a popular snorkeling and diving destination.

Get in[edit]
At least one daily boat leaves Ai Island in early morning for Banda Neira, then returns from Neira Island to Ai Island around 11:00-12:00.

Get around[edit]
On foot or you may ask the locals to ride you by motorbike or rent their motorbike.

See[edit]
Fort Revengie
A beach in Pulau Ai
White sand beaches, fort, and plantations.

Do[edit]
Snorkelling or scuba diving.

Buy[edit]
There are only few small convenience stores selling cigarettes and other usual stuff.

Eat[edit]
All guesthouses include 3 meals a day.

Drink[edit]
There is nowhere to get alcohol on this island.

Connect[edit]
No Internet is available here (no Wifi, and cellphone data does not work).

Go next[edit]
You may charter a boat to go to Pulau Run, but using public transport you'll have to track back to Banda Neira.
