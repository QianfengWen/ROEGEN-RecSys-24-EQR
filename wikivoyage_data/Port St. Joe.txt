
Port St. Joe is a city on the Forgotten Coast of Florida.


Get in[edit]
The closest airports in the area are the Apalachicola Municipal airport (AAF IATA) located about 17 miles southeast of Port St Joe in Apalachicola, Florida, the Panama City Northwest Florida International Airport (ECP IATA) located approximately 60 miles northwest of Port St Joe in Panama City, Florida, the Tallahassee Regional airport (TLH IATA) located roughly 69 miles northeast of Port St Joe in Tallahassee, Florida, and finally the Marianna Municipal airport (FAA LID: MAI) located around 71 miles north of Port St Joe in Marianna, Florida.

Get around[edit]
The preferred mode of transportation in Port St. Joe is the golf cart.

Sleep[edit]
29.77668-85.284241 MainStay Suites, 3951 East Hwy 98, ☏ +1 850 229-6246. Check-in: 4pm, check-out: 11am. The only AAA-rated hotel in Gulf County, located 15 minutes from historic Apalachicola, and one of only four along the entire Forgotten Coast to receive a 3-diamond rating. $99. 
29.81155-85.303072 The Port Inn, 501 Monument Ave, ☏ +1 850-229-7678. Check-in: 4pm, check-out: 11am. Port Inn is a restored 21-room hotel located in the small coastal town of Port St. Joe on Florida's quiet Gulf Coast, less than twenty minutes from Apalachicola and Mexico Beach. The Inn sits just across the street from St. Joseph Bay and Frank Pate Park with public boat launching facilities, and just three blocks from a new, state-of-the-art marina offering fishing charters and waterfront dining. $99.
