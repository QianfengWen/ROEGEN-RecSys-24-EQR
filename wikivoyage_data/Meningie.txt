
Meningie is a town on the south-east side of Lake Albert in South Australia.


Understand[edit]
Meningie has an abundance of attractions and amenities for travellers. The lake provides visitors with many scenic locations place to fish, swim, kayak, jet ski, water ski, or wake-board, and is the closest township to the Coorong National Park, which attracts thousands of visitors annually from all over the world.

Get in[edit]
The town is only accessible by car, as there's no trains or buses that run to Meningie. From Adelaide, take the M1, A1 and then turn onto B1 at Tailem Bend. Then continue onto Meningie road for about 54km, and then, you've arrived onto Meningie.
