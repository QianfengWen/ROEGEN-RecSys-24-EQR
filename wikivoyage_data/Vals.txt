
Vals is a village and valley in Graubunden, in the Surselva district.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
0°0′0″N 0°0′0″EMap of Vals
The closest train station is in Ilanz, with trains from Chur. From there a postal bus[dead link] runs hourly to Vals (36 minutes).

Get around[edit]
Vals is manageable on foot. During the winter season, there is a free shuttle[dead link] bus running through the village.

See[edit]
