
Narberth (Welsh: Arberth) is in Pembrokeshire.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
South of the A40 between Haverfordwest and Carmarthen.
The train between Swansea and Pembroke stops at Narberth on request.

Arriva Trains Wales
Bus available from Cardigan.
