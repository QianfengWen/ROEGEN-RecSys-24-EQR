
Taipa-Mangonui is a string of seaside communities in the Far Far North of the North Island of New Zealand. The communities lie along State Highway 10 in the large Doubtless Bay and are, from the west, Taipa, Cable Bay, Coopers Beach and Mangonui. There are also smaller settlements on the more remote Karikari Peninsula, which forms the western end of Doubtless Bay.

Get in[edit]
By bus[edit]
Mangonui, Coopers Beach, Cable Bay and Taipa are each served by a daily Intercity coach that departs Kerikeri at 12:35PM and takes about an hour. There are also services from Kaitaia leaving at 8AM and 12:15PM. There are connections from Auckland.
Far North Link runs a weekday commuter service leaving Mangonui at 6:55AM for Kaitaia, then leaving Kaitaia to return at 5:05PM, with additional services on Tu.
