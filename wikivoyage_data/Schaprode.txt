
Schaprode is a village on the island of Rügen.


Understand[edit]
Schaprode Harbour
The small harbour is one of the access points to the island of Hiddensee with a 54.51327113.1634621 ferry and water taxis.
As no cars are allowed on Hiddensee many people drive to Schaprode then go by foot or bike from here. The village has basically become a large car park.

Get in[edit]
The village is 24 km from Bergen auf Rügen by country roads. Alternatively by boat from Hiddensee.
