
Paluma is a very small village 60 km north of Townsville, Queensland, Australia. 


Understand[edit]
If you want to escape the hot, humid weather of Townsville, go up to Paluma, which is in the Mount Spec ranges of Thuringowa's heritage-listed Wet Tropics.

Get in[edit]
18°56′24″S 146°9′36″EMap of Paluma
To get in by car, take the bitumen road from the Bruce Highway all the way to Paluma. The view is spectacular as you wind your way up to the 1000 metres above sea level where Paluma is located.
