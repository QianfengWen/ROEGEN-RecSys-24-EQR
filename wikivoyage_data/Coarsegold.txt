
Coarsegold is a city in Madera County in the Sierra Nevada region of California.


Get in[edit]
37°15′44″N 119°42′4″WMap of Coarsegold
By car[edit]
By car, the best access to Coarsegold is taking California State Route 41 north from Fresno.

By bus[edit]
 Yosemite Area Regional Transit System (YARTS). May 14 to September 14, seven days a week. The YARTS shuttle travels on Highway 41 between Fresno and Wawona and Yosemite Valley, with stops in Coarsegold at Coarsegold Market and Chukchansi Gold Resort & Casino. Ticket prices include park entry, and can be purchased from the shuttle driver. (updated Feb 2016)
Madera County Connection's Eastern Madera County route provides limited service from Madera Monday–Friday.
