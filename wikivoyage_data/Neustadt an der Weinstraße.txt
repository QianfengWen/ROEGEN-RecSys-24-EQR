Neustadt an der Weinstraße is a city at the German Wine Route in the district Südliche Weinstraße in the state Rhineland-Palatinate.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
Frankfurt airport, Stuttgart airport and Saarbrücken airport are all about 2½ hours away.

By train[edit]
49.3495678.1403891 Neustadt (Weinstraße) Hauptbahnhof. It is a key intersection point of a number of rail lines so has good direct connections to major cities in the region such as Frankfurt, Mannheim, Kaiserslautern, Homburg, Saarbrücken as well as further afield to Darmstadt, Dresden, Leipzig, Erfurt. Local connections to Homburg–Osterburken (S1) and Kaiserslautern–Mosbach (S2) as well as Ludwigshafen and Landau in der Pfalz. 
By car[edit]
The town lies just of the A65, about 30 km from Ludwigshafen and 50 km from Karlsruhe.
