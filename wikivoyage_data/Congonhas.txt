

Statue by Aleijadinho in at Bom Jesus do Matosinhos Sanctuary
Congonhas is a city in Minas Gerais, Brazil, also known as Congonhas do Campo.


Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
BR-040 - south from Belo Horizonte, or north from Conselheiro Lafaiete, Juiz de Fora, Petrópolis and Rio de Janeiro
BR-383 - north from São João del Rei
MG-443 - west from Ouro Branco and Ouro Preto
