Stafford is the county town of Staffordshire. It is a market town.


Get in[edit]
52°48′24″N 2°7′2″WMap of Stafford
By train[edit]
52.80359-2.123071 Stafford station.    is on the West Coast Mainline and is served with trains from London Euston, Birmingham, Manchester and the North West and Bristol and the South West.

By car[edit]
Stafford is close to both the M6 (junctions 13 and 14) and M6 Toll road and is very accessible by car. The A34, A449, and A518 pass through the town.

By plane[edit]
You can fly into either Birmingham Airport (BHX IATA) or Manchester Airport (MAN IATA) and then connect via road or rail to Stafford.
