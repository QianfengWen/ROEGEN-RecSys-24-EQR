
Manokwari is a city of 107,000 people (2018) in Papua. Manokwari is the capital city of West Papua province, and a hub city.


Understand[edit]
Manokwari City
Climate[edit]
Manokwari is three meters above sea level. Its average temperature is 26.3 °C (79.3 °F). It receives 2,597 mm (102.2 in) of rain annually.

Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
-0.893563134.0504371 Rendani Airport (MKW  IATA). There are several daily flights from Jakarta's Soekarno-Hatta International Airport on Sriwijaya Air, Lion Air, and Batik Air. Some flights make a stopover in Sorong and Makassar.   
By boat[edit]
PELNI boats, KM. Sinabung and KM. Labobar stop in Manokwari from Surabaya and Makassar.

By car[edit]
You can reach Manokwari by road Trans Papua from Sorong with a travel time of approximately 12 hours.

Get around[edit]
Map of Manokwari

You can get around by angkot (minivan) or ojek (motorcycle taxi).

Fishing[edit]
You may fish on the sea by renting a boat or visiting some fishing ponds located in Prafi, Manokwari. You may find the fishing pond by searching the location with prefix pemancingan.
