
Pekalongan is a city on the north coast of Central Java.


Understand[edit]
Batik tulis (that is, with hand-drawn motifs) Jawa Hokokai from Pekalongan
The seat of the Pekalongan regency, today it is one of the most important port cities in Central Java, Indonesia. Pekalongan is known for its batik and is listed as one of the UNESCO Creative Cities in the category "Crafts and Folk Arts".

Get in[edit]
Map of Pekalongan (city)


By car[edit]
Pekalongan is along the North Coast of Great Post Road or well-known as Jalur Pantura.

By train[edit]
-6.88986109.664271 Pekalongan railway station is served by regular trains to the west (Tegal, Cirebon, and Jakarta) and to the east (Semarang and Surabaya). There is one daily train to Bandung.

By ride-hailing app[edit]
There are some online ride-hailing apps such as Gojek. You may choose to go by motorcycle or car.

Eat[edit]
Tauto
There is a soto dish from Pekalongan called tauto.

-6.884813109.6723131 Nasi Megono RM Tjukup, Jl. Manggis sebelah PLN Pekalongan No.10, Sampangan, East Pekalongan, ☏ +62 877 567 77798. 7AM-4PM. Nasi megono is a typical rice dish of Pekalongan Regency made from pieces of young jackfruit with a blend of grated coconut. It is usually served with mendoan, black cooked squid, balado egg, tempeh orek, cucumber, and chili sauce. (updated Sep 2022)
