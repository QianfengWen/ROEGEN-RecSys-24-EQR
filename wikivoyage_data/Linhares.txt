
Linhares is in the state of Espírito Santo, Brazil.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
BR-101 enters the city from Campos dos Goytacazes and Rio de Janeiro in the south-southwest and Guarapari, Vila Velha and Vitória in the northeast.
Get around[edit]
See[edit]
 Lagoa Juparanã. Brazil's second largest lake in terms of water volume. Public beaches include Praia das Três Pontas, Praia do Caju, and Praia do Minotauro.
