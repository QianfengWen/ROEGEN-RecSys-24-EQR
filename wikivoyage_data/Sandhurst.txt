
Sandhurst is a village in Berkshire. Probably best known as the home of the Royal Military Academy Sandhurst (often referred to simply as "Sandhurst", "The Academy" or "The RMA").


Understand[edit]
The village and adjoining facilities tend to merge into the adjacent Surrey town of Camberley.
