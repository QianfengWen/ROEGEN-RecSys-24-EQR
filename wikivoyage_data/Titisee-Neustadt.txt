
Titisee-Neustadt is a spa town of 12,000 people (2020) on the north shore of Lake Titisee, in the Black Forest.

Get in[edit]
Train from Freiburg, 2 per hour to Titisee station, 1 per hour to Neustadt.

Get around[edit]
Map of Titisee-Neustadt
Titisee and Neustadt are both rather compact, but despite sharing a mayor, they are too far apart to easily walk, so you should take a bike or bus between the two.

Do[edit]
Walking is the principal activity in summer, with a network of well marked routes. Usually you will find an inn somewhere along the route!
Skiing takes over in winter, as long as there is enough snow.
