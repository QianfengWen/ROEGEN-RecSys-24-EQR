Agios Ioannis, Mouresi, Kissos, Anilio, Papa Nero and Ntamouchari are in Northeast Pelion in Greece. Since these villages are close to each other, they are considered to be one travel destination.

Get in[edit]
There are frequently buses from Volos Interurban Bus Terminal ("Yperastiko KTEL") (KTEL Volou) to most of the villages in that area. Details on buses, can be found online here

Sleep[edit]
 Kentikon Bungalows, Agios Ioannis (30m from the beach and seaside road, behind tavern named Kyma). Great place to stay. Friendly staff and superb location. Very good homemade breakfast. €75-100 for a two-bed room. 
 Irida Studios (150 m from the beach). Irida Studios is at a higher level of the village. The accommodation consists of 8 studios in fresh, bright colours.
