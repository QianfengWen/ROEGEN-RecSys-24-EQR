
Almeida is a town and municipality in Beira Alta, Portugal. The border town of Vilar Formoso is in the municipality.


Understand[edit]
Building at the Spanish border in Vilar Formoso.

Almeida (/ɐlˈmɐjðɐ/, ahl-MAY-thuh) municipality has over 7,200 people (2011), while the town itself has about 1,300. The town of Vilar Formoso within the municipality serves as a major border crossing, across from Fuentes de Oñoro, Spain. As a result of the Schengen Agreement, the border is open.

Get in[edit]
Almeida and Vilar Formoso are on the A25 motorway (Autoestrada das Beiras Litoral e Alta).

40.6061-6.829831 Vilar Formoso Railway Station (Estação Ferroviária de Vilar Formoso). PDF timetable. There are two regional trains daily from Guarda. Before the pandemic, there was daily international service through Vilar Formoso from Lisbon, Coimbra, Madrid, and Irun/Hendaye (on the Spain–France border) on the Sud Expresso and Lusitânia Comboio Hotel trains.  (updated Jun 2021)
