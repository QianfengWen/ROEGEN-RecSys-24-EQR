
Lenti[dead link] is a town in Zala County in the valley of Kerka river, near to the border with Slovenia.


Understand[edit]
46°37′23″N 16°32′17″EMap of Lenti

The earliest traces of the town are from the Roman age. It was probably an inhabited settlement, which is also proved by the proximity of the Amber Road. Lenti was first mentioned in 1237 as "Nemith". However, the castle built beside the settlement at the beginning of the 13th century had more importance. Lenti was elevated to town status in  1978.
This article includes travel-related information not only for Lenti, but also for the surrounding rural areas.

Get in[edit]
By train[edit]
Trains are available from Zalaegerszeg four times a day. It takes one hour and costs 630 Ft. From Budapest you have to transfer in Zalaegerszeg. The whole journey from Budapest takes about 4.5 hours and costs 4660 Ft.
46.621316.52961 Lenti Train Station, Táncsics Mihály utca 1, ☏ +36 1 349-4949, informacio@mav-start.hu. There is no ticket office, tickets can be purchased on the train. The train station is close to the center. The bus line 1A goes between the train station and the regional bus station.  (updated Oct 2018)

By bus[edit]
Direct buses from Budapest-Népliget are available once a day on weekdays and twice a day on weekends. The journey takes 4 hours and costs 4840 Ft. More frequent buses are available from Zalaegerszeg. The journey takes one hour and costs 840 Ft.
46.6221816.541052 Lenti Bus Station, Ifjúság útja 1, ☏ +36 92 351-038, info@volanbusz.hu. Ticket office: M–F 08:00–16:00. (updated Oct 2018)

By bus[edit]
Volánbusz operates local buses in Lenti. Ticket (in 2019): 205 Ft, pass (in 2019): 4010 Ft per month. Tickets can be purchased at the bus station (only on weekdays) and at the driver. Public transport map.

By narrow-gauge train[edit]
Csömödér State Forest Railway (Csömödéri Állami Erdei Vasút) operates narrow-gauge trains between Lenti and Kistolmács. The trains are operated only between April 21 and September 29 from Monday to Saturday. The ticket costs for adult: 300 Ft–1400 Ft; for student, senior: 250 Ft–900 Ft (depending on the distance).
