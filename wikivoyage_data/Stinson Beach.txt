
Stinson Beach is a beach town in Marin County, California.


Get in[edit]
To reach Stinson Beach by car, take Highway 1.
If you'd prefer to take the bus, Marin Transit's West Marin Stagecoach route 61 offers somewhat inconvenient service from Sausalito and Marin City, where you can make connections from San Rafael and other parts of the Bay Area.
