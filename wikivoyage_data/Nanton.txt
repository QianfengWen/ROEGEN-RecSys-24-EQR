
Nanton is a town of 2,100 (2016) in Southern Alberta.  Traditionally a farming and ranching community, Nanton is now a popular tourist destination for its historic main Street and museums.


Understand[edit]
Downtown Nanton
Nanton was historically known as "Tap Town", after providing passing motorists with free water supplied from the foothills via a stand tap on the northbound highway. This water was one of this first to be bottled and sold in Canada, resulting in the creation of Nanton Water & Soda Ltd, still a thriving business in the town today.

Get around[edit]
50°20′58″N 113°46′18″WMap of Nanton
Nanton is fairly compact and easily walkable. Highway 2 splits into parallel one-way streets when passing the town, and the historic business district is located between the two streets.
