
Consdorf is a town in the Luxembourgian Mullerthal region.

Get in[edit]
Stairs connecting to the Burgkapp panoramic point.
By bus[edit]
Bus 111 runs every hour from Luxembourg City directly to Consdorf. The bus runs once an hour on weekdays and once every two hours on Saturday. The bus leaves from Luxembourg City at the railway station (Gare Centrale). It also stops in the city centre at Badanstalt.

By car[edit]
Consdorf is located about 3km off the N11 (E29) road, which connects Luxembourg City with Echternach. From Luxembourg, simply follow the signs for Consdorf. Coming from Echternach follow the signs directing to Scheidgen and Larochette. Once having reached Scheidgen, keep going straight to get to Consdorf.

Get around[edit]
Consdorf can be travelled on foot, but travelling by bike or car is recommended due to the steep terrain.

Do[edit]
Two cyclists by the church of Consdorf.
Hiking - Consdorf is located right by the Mullerthal trail. Route C5 and C6 can be found just north of the town.
Cycling - The smooth roads in Echternach make for great cycling routes. Consdorf also connects to the bike-network of the Echternach Canton.
Buy[edit]
Supermarkets and other shops can be found in Echternach. Another choice is to travel to Mersch and visit the Topaze shopping centre.
