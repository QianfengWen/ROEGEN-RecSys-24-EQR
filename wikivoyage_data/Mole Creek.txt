
Mole Creek is a small township on the North West Coast region of Tasmania.

Pioneer Drive, Mole Creek

Understand[edit]
Mole Creek is located close to the Walls of Jerusalem National Park.
The main attraction of Mole Creek is the extensive karst system, one a few of which are open to tourists.

Get in[edit]
Driving is the easiest option to get into Mole Creek and surrounding areas via the B12 Mole Creek Road from Deloraine. Beware, the roads are quite windy and can be narrow in places.
