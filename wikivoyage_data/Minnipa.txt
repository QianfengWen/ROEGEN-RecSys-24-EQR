
Minnipa is a town in South Australia, the gateway to the Eyre Peninsula.


Understand[edit]
Minnipa is a small town on the Eyre Highway (A1), where is crosses the base of the Eyre Peninsula.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Minnipa can be reached when travelling along the Eyre Highway (A1)

By train[edit]
Minnipa does have a train station with occasional trains to Adelaide

Get around[edit]
See[edit]
Pildappa Rock is an interesting wave rock formation 15km outside of Minnipa (along a good quality dirt road).  Follow the signs from the town.
