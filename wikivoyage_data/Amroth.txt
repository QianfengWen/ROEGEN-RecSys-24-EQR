
Amroth (Welsh: Amroth or Llanrhath) is village and holiday resort in Pembrokeshire.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
18 miles south west of Carmarthen off the A477
The 351 bus can be used from Pendine, Saundersfoot and Tenby.

A unique and spectacular holiday destination, Amroth Castle occupies spacious, green grounds right where the Pembrokeshire Coast National Park meets the sparkling sands and inviting waters of Amroth Beach.
