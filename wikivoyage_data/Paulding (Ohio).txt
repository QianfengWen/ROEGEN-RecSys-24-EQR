
Paulding is a village of 3,600 people (2020) in Northwest Ohio. Paulding is largely a bedroom community: many residents commute to industrial jobs, often in neighboring counties. It has an historic county courthouse surrounded by a traditional town square with quaint shops and family restaurants.


Understand[edit]
Paulding is the seat of Paulding County, Ohio, and is also the largest village in the county.
