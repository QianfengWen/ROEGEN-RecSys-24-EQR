
Malabar, Florida is in Brevard County.


Understand[edit]
Malabar is a small, friendly town with almost 3,000 people.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
From State Road 514, which leads into Palm Bay. US-1 goes through the town from north to south, connecting Malabar to towns such as Melbourne, Sebastian, and Titusville.

Get around[edit]
You can rent a car at Melbourne International Airport.

Go next[edit]
Palm Bay and Melbourne are the main towns in the immediate vicinity.
U.S. Highway 1 follows the entirety of the U.S. eastern coast, continuing south from Malabar to Miami and the Florida Keys, and north to Maine.
