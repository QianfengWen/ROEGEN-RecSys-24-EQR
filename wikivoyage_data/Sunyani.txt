
Sunyani is the regional capital of the Bono region.


Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
The Sunyani Domestic Airport is served by the Kotoka International Airport at Accra, and there are daily domestic flights to Sunyani.

Get around[edit]
Sunyani seems to have been designed by very thoughtful town planners; the layout of the main roads makes sense and it's easy to get your bearings.  Shared taxis trawl back and forth along most major streets and are a fast and inexpensive way to move around town.
