


Not to be confused with Salvador e Santa Maria da Feira (Beja).

Santa Maria da Feira is a city in the Douro Litoral region of Northern Portugal. It is a city and dense populated municipality, in the Porto metropolitan area, belonging to Aveiro district.


Understand[edit]
The castle.

Santa Maria da Feira (/ˈsɐ̃tɐ mɐˈɾi.ɐ ðɐ ˈfɐjɾɐ/, SUHN-tuh muh-REE-uh thuh FAY-ruh) municipality has over 139,000 residents (2011), with over 18,000 in the city proper.

Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
Porto Int. Airport (OPO IATA).
By train[edit]
Local trains run from Espinho and Aveiro via the Linha do Vouga line.

By car[edit]
Santa Maria da Feira is served by the A1 (Autoestrada do Norte), A29 (Autoestrada da Costa de Prata), A32 (Autoestrada de Entre-Douro-e-Vouga), and A41 (CREP - Circular Regional Exterior do Porto) motorways.

By bus[edit]
Intercity bus service is available from Rede Expressos, Transdev, and União de Transportes dos Carvalhos (UTC).
