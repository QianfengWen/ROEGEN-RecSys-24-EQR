
Cape Jervis is a town in South Australia, and a gateway point to Kangaroo Island, Australia's third largest island excluding the mainland.


Get in[edit]
Sealink operate coach services from Adelaide twice daily, and to Victor Harbor once daily except Saturdays and Wednesdays.  There is no other scheduled commercial transport options to Cape Jervis.
Rental car facilities are available in Victor Harbor and in Lonsdale. The harbor and wharf is around 1 km (0.62 mi) from the town.

Get around[edit]
If you were to only get around the inner areas of the town's CBD, then it's best to walk, but otherwise take the car.
