
Santa Maria de Jetibá is a town in Espírito Santo, known for its Pomeranian heritage.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
ES-080 - 70 km from Vitória
Get around[edit]
See[edit]
 Canaã Valley (Vale do Canaã). 
 Pastor Falls (Cachoeira do Pastor). 
 Pedra do Garrafão. Highest point of the city, at 1300 m in altitude. 
 Pomeranian Immigration Museum (Museu da Imigração Pomerana), Rua Dalmácio Espíndola, 260, ☏ +55 27 3263-2727. Tu-Su 9AM-11AM and 12:30PM-5PM. 
 Rio Bonito Reservoir. 
 São Sebastião do Meio Falls (Cachoeira de São Sebastião do Meio). 
 Timber frame architecture. Due to its Pomeranian immigration, many of the buildings have a post-and-beam construction. 
Do[edit]
Festa Pomerana, in May. Pomeranian heritage festival.
