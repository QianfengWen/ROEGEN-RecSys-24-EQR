
Fredericksburg is a village in Wayne County in Northeast Ohio. The population was 487 at the 2000 census.


Get in[edit]
Map of Fredericksburg (Ohio)
Very close to the traditional Ohio Amish tourist area of Holmes County.
Routes 501(north), 201 (south), 192 (southwest), and 94a/2 (northeast) merge in the center of town.

Get around[edit]
Drive, bike, walk, or carriage.
