Buy[edit]
Lotus Ware in the Metropolitan Museum of Art
 Lotus Ware. A type of porcelain that was produced in East Liverpool. While production stopped in the late 1890s that quality was considered high, and surviving pieces are valued as a piece of East Liverpool history.   (updated Nov 2018)
