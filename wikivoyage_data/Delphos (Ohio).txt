
Delphos is in Northwest Ohio. It is split between Van Wert County and Allen County.


Understand[edit]
Delphos is a city of over 7,000. It was founded in the 1830s and was named after the Greek mythological figure Delphus.

By Car[edit]
Delphos lies on US30. SR66 and SR 190 also pass through Delphos.
