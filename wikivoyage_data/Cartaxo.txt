
Cartaxo is a city and municipality in Ribatejo, Portugal. The municipality calls itself Capital do Vinho, "Capital of Wine".


Understand[edit]
Pontével.

Cartaxo (kuhr-TAH-shoo, /kɐɾ.ˈta.ʃu/) municipality has 23,211 residents (2021 preliminary), with just over 10,000 living in the city. The municipality contains two towns: Pontével and Vila Chã de Ourique.

Get in[edit]
39.113611-8.7780561 Estação Ferroviária do Setil (Setil Railway Station). PDF timetable. Setil is the main train station in the municipality. It's served by regional trains along the Linha do Norte (Northern Line) from Lisbon, Entroncamento, and Tomar.  (updated Sep 2021)
Intercity bus service is provided by Rede Expressos.
Rodoviária do Tejo offers regional bus service, including the Rápida Laranja fast line from Lisbon and Santarém.
Cartaxo is served by the A1 motorway (Autoestrada do Norte) from Lisbon, Porto, Coimbra, Aveiro, Leiria, and Santarém. The older EN3 road also passes through the city.
