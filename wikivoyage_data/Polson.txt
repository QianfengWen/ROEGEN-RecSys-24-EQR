
Polson is a town of 5,100 people (2020) in Montana. 


Understand[edit]
Polson is on the Flathead Indian Reservation.  It is at the south end of Flathead Lake, the largest natural freshwater lake west of the Mississippi.

Get in[edit]
Polson is an hour north of Missoula and an hour south of Kalispell.  It is on Highway 93.  It is accessed from exit 96 on Interstate 90.

Get around[edit]
Map of Polson
While Polson is a smaller town, most of the businesses are along the highway. There isn't a centralized "downtown" shop area so using a car or bicycle is best.
