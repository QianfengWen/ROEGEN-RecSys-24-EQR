

Diamantina
Diamantina is a colonial city in Northern Minas Gerais, Brazil. Its 17th-century streets and buildings are witnesses to the country's diamond and gold digging ages, and its historic center has been listed as a World Heritage Site by UNESCO.


Get in[edit]

By bus[edit]
The bus station (rodoviária) is at Largo Dom João, 134. (38) 3531-9176. There are frequent buses from Belo Horizonte by companies São Geraldo[dead link] and Pássaro Verde (websites in Portuguese only).
There is also one daily arrival from São Paulo.
