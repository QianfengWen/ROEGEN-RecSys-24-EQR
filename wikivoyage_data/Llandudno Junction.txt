
Llandudno junction (Welsh: Cyffordd Llandudno) is a town in Conwy county borough, in North Wales.


Understand[edit]
Llandudno junction is separated from Llandudno by Deganwy. It is about an hours walk from Llandudno.

Get in[edit]
By train[edit]
53.284-3.8091 Llandudno Junction railway station. on the Crewe to Holyhead North Wales Coast Line and the Llandudno to Blaenau Ffestiniog Conwy Valley Line   (updated Oct 2016)
Town is on intersection of the A55 truck road Chester - Holyhead, also known as the North Wales Expressway (Welsh: Gwibffordd Gogledd Cymru), and the A470 Cardiff to Glan Conwy Trunk Road 3 miles south of Llandudno.

Get around[edit]
Conway Road is the main road. The Wales Coast Path runs through too.

Do[edit]
The town is home to a 9-screen multiplex Cineworld cinema, Llandudno Junction Leisure Centre, ASDA, Tesco and Lidl supermarkets, and a McDonald's, KFC and Pizza Hut restaurant.
Llandudno Junction also has a number of beautiful walks and a fantastic bird sanctuary at RSPB Conwy.
