
Abrantes is a city in Portugal's Ribatejo province. It lies on the River Tagus (Rio Tejo).


Understand[edit]
Aquapolis in Abrantes.

Abrantes (uh-BRUHN-t(ih)sh, /ɐ.ˈbɾɐ̃.tɨʃ/) municipality has 34,336 residents (2021 provisional), with about 17,500 living in the city proper.

Get in[edit]
By train[edit]
CP – Comboios de Portugal serves Abrantes via the Beira Baixa line.

By bus[edit]
Regional bus service is provided by Rodoviária do Tejo, and long-distance intercity coaches are operated by Rede Expressos.

By car[edit]
Abrantes is served by the A23 motorway (Autoestrada da Beira Interior).
