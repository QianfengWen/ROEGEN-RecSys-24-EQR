
Kidderminster is in Worcestershire. Whilst not your typical tourist destination, you may end up here to visit relatives or the like. Mostly harmless, it can boast of having a lock.

See[edit]
52.384244-2.2396871 Severn Valley Railway (Kidderminster Station), 1 Comberton Place, ☏ +44 1299 403816, contact@svrlive.com. 9AM-5PM. The Severn Valley Railway operates full-size, mainly steam-hauled passenger trains along the beautiful Severn Valley between Kidderminster, Bewdley and Bridgnorth. Stop off at The Engine House, the Visitor Centre at Highley, to discover the collection of steam locomotives, themed exhibitons, and shop, restaurant and outdoor play area. Country stations at Arley, Highley and Hampton Loade are well worth a visit. A train ride not enough? Then how about a Footplate Experience where you can drive a steamtrain.   
52.384694-2.2455232 The Museum of Carpet, Stour Vale Mill, Green Street, DY10 1AZ, ☏ +44 1562 69028, collections@museumofcarpet.org.uk. Kidderminster is well known for its manufacturing of carpets and this museum reflects that. (updated Oct 2015)
52.3391-2.2423 Hartlebury Castle, Hartlebury, DY11 7XZ, ☏ +44 1299 250416. Modernised mid-13th century as a fortified manor house and museum.   (updated Oct 2016)
52.406434-2.1712654 Harborough Hall, Birmingham Rd, Blakedown, DY10 3LG. This timber-frame, stucoed house dates back to around 1635. It once belonged to the family of William Shenstone the poet, is probably the successor of a house that goes back to Medieval times.
