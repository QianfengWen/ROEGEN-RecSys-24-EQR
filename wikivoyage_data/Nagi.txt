Nagi (奈義町 Nagi-chō) is a town in Okayama Prefecture, Japan.


Understand[edit]
Tourist information sites[edit]
The local tourist association has a bilingual guide site.
The town has an official Japanese-only guide site with integrated Google Translate.


Get in[edit]
There are no train stations in Nagi, but it can easily be reached by taking a Chutetsu Bus from Tsuyama. The bus station is across the street from Tsuyama Station with buses departing hourly for Nagi.

See[edit]
35.123945134.1749331 Nagi MOCA (奈義町現代美術館), ☏ +81 86-836-5811. 09:30-17:00. The Nagi Museum of Contemporary Art (Nagi MOCA) is a world-class museum of modern art in which the entire museum was constructed for and around the art within. ¥700. 
35.156121134.2030742 Bodai-ji (菩提寺), 1532 Koen. 24h. Bodaiji Temple is most known for its gigantic 900 year old gingko tree. Its circumference is 12 meters. It's the kind of site that can really make a person feel small. It's especially beautiful in the fall when the leaves turn yellow. The actual temple building is rather small and nondescript, but those with an appreciation of nature should be quite at peace here. Free. 
35.101026134.1872483 Nagi Vicarya Museum (なぎビカリアミュージアム), ☏ +81 86-836-3977. A museum that features 16 million year old Vicarya fossils excavated from Nagi. It also has information about other fossils and archaeology.

Eat[edit]
Okayama is one of only a few prefectures in Japan to raise its own black pigs, with the brand being Okayama black pigs (おかやま黒豚). Although the prefecture is credited in the name, Nagi is in fact the only place in the prefecture that raises these pigs. They've been raised here since the Meiji Period.

35.123272134.1749851 PIZZERIA La Gita (ラジータ), ☏ +81 86-820-1171. A popular pizzeria with the approval of the Associazione Verace Pizza Napoletana, an organization based in Naples, that promotes pizzerias that serve authentic Neapolitan pizza. In addition to their pizzas, they also have a variety of pasta dishes and salads. (updated Aug 2021)
