

Botucaraí Ridge, in Candelária
Candelária[dead link] is a town in the Central East of Rio Grande do Sul. It is part of the Paleorrota Geopark.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
RS-287 (Dinosaur Highway) - east from Santa Maria or west from Novo Hamburgo
RS-400 from the north or RS-410 from the south
Get around[edit]
See[edit]
Aristides Carlos Rodrigues Museum
 Aqueduct. 304-meter-long brick aqueduct built in 1862. 
-29.672358-52.7879631 Aristides Carlos Rodrigues Museum (Museu Aristides Carlos Rodrigues), Av. Pereira Rego, 1000, ☏ +55 51 3743-8171. This museum has paleonotology and archeology exhibits of fossils and items found in the area, as well as an exhibit on German colonization of the town. 
 Botucaraí Ridge (Santo Cerro). This mountain ridge, approximately 570 meters high, receives religious pilgrimages at Good Friday, Easter, and Christmas. 
 Carlos Larger Beach (Praia Carlos Larger). Popular gathering spot during the summer months. 
 Ferradura Falls (Cascata da Ferradura), Roncador. A secluded 52-meter waterfall on the Roncador Brook. 
-29.543605-52.8174752 Imperial Bridge (Ponte do Império). Stone bridge on the Botucaraí Way, an important road during the Imperial Era. Built in 1879-1880.
