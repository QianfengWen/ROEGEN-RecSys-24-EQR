
Deerfield Township is a township in Warren County, Southwest Ohio. Deerfield Township is an unincorporated area outside of the City of Mason. Locally, the administrative bodies of Mason and Deerfield Township have historically shown animosity towards each other.


Get in[edit]
(As reckoned by the Deerfield Township Administrative Building)

By car[edit]
From Interstate I-71, take the Fields-Ertel exit.  Take Mason-Montgomery Road north to Parkway Drive.  The Administrative Building is on the East.

By plane[edit]
Deerfield Township is situated between three major airports:

John Glenn Columbus International Airport, Columbus, Ohio (CMH IATA)
Cincinnati-Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG IATA)
James M Cox Dayton International Airport, Dayton, Ohio)[dead link] (DAY IATA)
Each airport can be accessed from Deerfield Township with at least some portion of interstate driving.
Estimated driving times from Deerfield Township to the airports are:

Deerfield Township to CVG: 45 minutes; I-71 South to I-75 South to I-275 West to Airport Exit.
Deerfield Township to CMH: 90 minutes; I-71 North to I-670 East to Airport Exit, Exit #9.
Deerfield Township to DAY: 60 minutes; Tylersville Road to I-75 North, through downtown Dayton, to I-70 West to Airport Exit.
