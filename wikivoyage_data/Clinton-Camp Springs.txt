Clinton and Camp Springs are unincorporated areas in Prince George's County, Maryland.



Get in[edit]
WMATA offers bus service albeit very infrequently. TheBus Prince George's County's local bus service offers better service but certainly the best form of getting to Clinton is a car. Branch Avenue is the nearest Metro station less than 10 mins away in car.

Get around[edit]
Car is the best way to get around Clinton. Bus service is very limited especially on weekends. The town is not very walkable.
