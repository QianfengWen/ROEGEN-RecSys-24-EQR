
Benalla is a town in Victoria in the High Country region along the Hume Freeway corridor.

Get in[edit]
36°33′23″S 145°59′10″EMap of Benalla

Benalla is a short diversion off the Hume Freeway between Wodonga and Melbourne.
Benalla has an airport, but there are no commercial scheduled flights.
NSW Trainlink services between Sydney and Melbourne stop here, as do V/Line services from Melbourne to Albury, making a total of five trains daily in each direction. V/Line also operate coach services to Bendigo or Adelaide via Shepparton, and Mulwala via Yarrawonga.

Get around[edit]
Local public transport within the town consists of a bus service operated by Benalla Bus Lines. The service consists of two loop routes that run hourly, with more services during peak times, and only on weekdays. Tickets for 2 hour, daily, weekly and monthly periods are available and all are also sold at concession rates (half price). The single ticket covers both lines.
Benalla Taxis operates a local taxi service which operates 24/7, bookings can be made over the phone (+61 3 5762 3333) and a taxi rank is available on Carrier Street near Bridge Street in the CBD.
