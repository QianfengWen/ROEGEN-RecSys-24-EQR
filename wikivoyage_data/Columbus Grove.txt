
Columbus Grove is a small city in Putnam County (Ohio).


Understand[edit]
Columbus Grove was founded in 1842 by settlers from Columbus Ohio.

Get in[edit]
Map of Columbus Grove
By Car[edit]
Columbus Grove lies a bit west of I75, and is also connected by SR 12 and SR 65.
