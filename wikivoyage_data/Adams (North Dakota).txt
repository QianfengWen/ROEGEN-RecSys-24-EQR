
Adams is a small town in the Red River Valley of North Dakota. It had a population of about 120 people in 2019.

Get in[edit]
Map of Adams (North Dakota)


By car: from Grand Forks, I-29 north to exit 176, then west on state highway 17 for about 40 miles.
