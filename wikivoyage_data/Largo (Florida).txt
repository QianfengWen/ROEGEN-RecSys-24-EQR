Largo is a city in Pinellas County in the Tampa Bay Area.


Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
The biggest airport in the nearby area is Tampa International Airport but some flights can land at St. Petersburg-Clearwater International Airport which is just east of the city, along Roosevelt Boulevard.

By car[edit]
I-275 comes into the area from Tampa. Alternately, US 19 comes into the city from the north.

By bus[edit]
The Pinellas Suncoast Transit Authority provides bus service from other cities in the area, including St. Petersburg, Clearwater, and Tampa.

Get around[edit]
By car[edit]
The main thoroughfares are Ulmerton Road and East Bay Drive going east and west, with Clearwater-Largo Road/Ridge Road, Missouri Avenue/Seminole Boulevard, Keene Road/Starkey Road, and Belcher Road going north and south.

By bus[edit]
The Pinellas Suncoast Transit Authority operates the buses for the whole of Pinellas County. Route map.
