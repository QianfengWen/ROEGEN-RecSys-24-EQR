Buy[edit]
Downtown Chagrin Falls boasts plenty of one of a kind boutiques and shops without the bustle of a city. Jewelry, clothing and shoes define the theme to many of the shops. Be sure to visit Mac's Tobacco Pouch for a cigar or, if for winter gear, head to Geiger's Ski & Sport Haus which is next door.

Sleep[edit]
 Inn of Chagrin Falls, 87 West St, ☏ +1 440 247-1200. Cozy unique rooms located only a block away from the center of town. 
 The Hillbrook Inn, 14800 Hillbrook Dr., ☏ +1 440 247-4940. A quaint bed and breakfast nestled in the woods by a picturesque river, Hillbrook also serves as a club. The Tudor mansion transports patrons back in time to 16th-century England and its character adds a touch of elegance to any trip. The Inn is very quiet and has very good food.
