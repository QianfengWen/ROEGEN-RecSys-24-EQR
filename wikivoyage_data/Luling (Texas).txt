
Luling is a city in the Post Oak Savannah region of Texas.


Get in[edit]
Interstate 10 at TX-80, between Houston and San Antonio.  Drive.

Get around[edit]
You're going to need a car.

See[edit]
 Water Melon Thump, ☏ +1 830-875-3214 ext 2, jamie@watermelonthump.com. Always on the last Thursday through Sunday of June. 
 Texas Water Safari. The second Saturday of June, comes through Luling Saturday afternoon to Sunday morning. 
 Zedler Mill Classic. A local festival with canoe races, food, music, and a beauty contest, held in April.  The park at the mill is open year round. 
Do[edit]
 Zedler Mill Paddling Trail. A gentle paddle down the San Marcos river.  Contact local outfitters for rentals.  Put in at SH-90 about five miles west of town, take out at Zedler Mill. free. 
 Luling Oil Museum, 421 E Davis St, ☏ +1 830-875-1922, fax: +1 830-875-2082, oilmuseum@austin.rr.com. Documenting the primary cause of Luling's size and survival. 
 Luling Golf Club, 1025 S Magnolia Ave, ☏ +1 830-875-5114, fax: +1 830-875-0209. Nine hole course. greens/cart: $25.
