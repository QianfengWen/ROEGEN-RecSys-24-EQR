

Millersburg
Millersburg is a city of 3,200 people (2019) in Wooster area Ohio that is located about midway between Cleveland and Columbus. Millersburg is the county seat of Holmes County


Understand[edit]
The Amish comprise nearly half of the county's population of 44,000), aking this region the largest Amish settlement in the world. The area also has many Mennonites in the population. When travelling to and driving in this area, be alert for the horse-drawn Amish buggies.
