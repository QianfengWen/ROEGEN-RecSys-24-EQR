


Brynsiencyn is a village on the island of Anglesey in north-west Wales.


Understand[edit]
It's in the parish of Llanidan on the Menai Strait.

Get in[edit]
On the A 4080, 6 miles south west of Menai Bridge.
The 42 bus from Bangor.
The closest rail station is Llanfairpwllgwyngyllgogerychwyrndrobwyllllantysiliogogogoch.
