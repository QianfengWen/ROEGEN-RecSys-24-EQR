
Ottumwa is a city in South Central Iowa.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Ottumwa is at the junction of US Highways 34 and 63.

By train[edit]
Amtrak serves Ottumwa via its California Zephyr route, running between Chicago to the San Francisco Bay Area.

41.0186-92.41491 Ottumwa railway station, 210 W Main St.   (updated Oct 2019)
By bus[edit]
Buses stop at the Amtrak station at 210 West Main St.
