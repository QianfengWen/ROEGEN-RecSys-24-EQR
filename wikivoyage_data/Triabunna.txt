
Triabunna is a scenic township on Tasmania's east coast.

Triabunna

Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
Your best way to get here from Hobart is to take the 1-hour drive onto the Tasman Highway (A3). The road is quite straightforward but does get windy in places.
