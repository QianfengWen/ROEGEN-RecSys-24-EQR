
Pellworm (North Frisian: Polweerm, Danish: Pelvorm) is one of the North Frisian Islands.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
The NPGD ferry from Strucklahnungshörn harbour on the Nordstrand peninsula.
The closest rail station on Husum.
