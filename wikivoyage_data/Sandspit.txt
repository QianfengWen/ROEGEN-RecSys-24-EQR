
Sandspit (Haida: K'il Kun) is only town on Moresby Island, Haida Gwaii, British Columbia. Sandspit is the main jumping off point for those flying in from Vancouver and to Haida Gwaii as well as for those wanting to explore Gwaii Haanas National Park Reserve.


Understand[edit]
Sandspit (population about 300 in 2016) is served by Sandspit Airport and by ferry from Alliford Bay to Skidegate Landing on Graham Island. Sandspit has accommodations, a campground, supermarket and 85-berth harbour serving visitors to Gwaii Haanas.

Do[edit]
Moresby Island Circle Tour. Follows an active gravel logging road past Skidegate Lake, Copper River, Gray Bay beach, and Copper Bay, where traditional Haida people fish for sockeye salmon and hang the fish out to dry in May and early June.
53.24288-131.812081 The Willows Golf Course, 342 Copper Bay Rd, ☏ +1 250-637-2388. A 9-hole golf course. (updated Jul 2020)
 Moresby Explorers, 365 Beach Road, ☏ +1 250-637-2215, toll-free: +1-800-806-7633, info@moresbyexplorers.com. Offers boat tours, kayaker services, and other marine transport. Owns Seaport B&B. (updated Jul 2020)
Other activities include beachcombing, hiking, fishing, boating, canoeing, flightseeing, camping and sea kayaking.
