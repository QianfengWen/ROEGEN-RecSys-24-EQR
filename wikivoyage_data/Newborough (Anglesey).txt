
Newborough (Welsh: Niwbwrch) is a village on the island of Anglesey in north-west Wales.


Understand[edit]
It's the closest village to Llanddwyn Island (Ynys Llanddwyn).

Get in[edit]
On the A4080 11 miles from Menai Bridge.
Bus 42[dead link] connects the village with Llangefni and Bangor.
