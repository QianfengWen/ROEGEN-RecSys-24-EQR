
Collarenebri is a village (population 650 in 2016) in Northwestern New South Wales.
Collarenebri is in the Walgett Shire Local Government Area.

By car[edit]
Collarenebri is about an hour's drive north east of Walgett via the B76 Gwydir Highway and an hour's drive west of Moree. From Mungindi, take the Gundabloui Road down south west for about an hour.
