
Chardon is the county seat of Geauga County.  It is in Ohio's "snowbelt" and receives an average of 106 inches of snow each winter.  In 1996, the National Guard was sent in after the city got about four feet of snow in less than a week.

By Car[edit]

Cleveland-Hopkins International Airport is a major international airport in Brook Park.It is about 50 minutes away.
