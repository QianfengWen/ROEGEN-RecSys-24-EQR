
Talisay (Cebu) is a town in Metro Cebu, just south of Cebu City and bordering it.


Understand[edit]
It is home to about 263,000 people (census, 1 May 2020) .

Get in[edit]
10°15′1″N 123°50′42″EMap of Talisay (Cebu)

The town is on the main ring highway that runs round the island, between Cebu city and Talisay this is named Cebu South Road.
The Cebu South Coast Road also connection the two urban areas, but closer to the coast.
MyBus have scheduled routes to Cebu.
Talisay is usually reached by bus, taxi or jeepney from Cebu City; see the Metro Cebu article for bus routes and other details.
The across-the-island highway (Wharf Road) to Toledo intersects the coastal highway at Talisay.
The port of Talisay has boats to and from Bohol.
