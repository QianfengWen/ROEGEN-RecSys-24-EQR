
Llanwrtyd Wells (Welsh: Llanwrtyd)  is a small town in Brecknockshire.


Understand[edit]
With a population of less than 800, LW claims to be the smallest community in the UK with the status of a town. It was awarded the Calor Village of the Year title for 2009 on account of its strong community spirit.

Get in[edit]
On the Heart of Wales railway line and A483 between Llandovery and Builth Wells.
