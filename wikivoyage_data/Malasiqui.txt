
Malasiqui is a municipality in Pangasinan.


Understand[edit]
Malasiqui has an agricultural economy centered on rice, corn, mangoes and vegetables.

Get in[edit]
First North Luzon Transit operate buses from San Carlos to Malasqui every hour.
