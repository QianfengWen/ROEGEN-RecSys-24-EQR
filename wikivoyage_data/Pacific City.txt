
Pacific City is a city on the Northern Oregon Coast.


Get in[edit]
Pacific City is located north of Lincoln City on the Oregon coast. The easiest way to get there is to take highway 101 which leads thru Pacific City. You can take a number of highways west to get to 101, depending on where you are located. Highways 22, 99 and 18 are the best choices for getting there from Salem or Portland.

45.2156-123.96961 Pelican Pub & Brewery, 33180 Cape Kiwanda Dr, ☏ +503 965-7007. Su.- Th.: 10:30AM-10PM; F-Sa.: 10:30AM-11PM. Offers a wide variety of meals for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
