See and do[edit]
The St. Benedict Church
Church of Bom Jesus do Saivá
 Fountain of the Carioca (Fonte da Carioca), Rua Theóphilo S. Gomes (next to Praça Carlos Gomes da Costa). Built in 1867. 
 Church of Bom Jesus do Saivá (Igreja de Bom Jesus do Saivá), Praça Carlos Cavalcanti (near the train station). Construction began on this church sometime between 1789 and 1817. 
 Church of Nossa Senhora do Pilar (Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Pilar). Founded in 1714. 
 Church of St. Benedict (Igreja de São Benedito), Rua Dr. Carlos Gomes da Costa. Served as a refuge for the slaves. 
 Train Station (Estação Ferroviária), ☏ +55 41 3432-4520. Built in 1916
