
Warragul is a small town located in Gippsland, Victoria.


Understand[edit]
Warragul is in the Shire of Baw Baw, a local government area in Victoria, Australia, in Gippsland. It has a population of approximately 14,276 people. 
It is one of the towns along the M1 Princes Freeway.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Warragul can be accessed by car when travelling along the M1 Princes Fwy (east). 

By train[edit]
V/Line operates train services from Melbourne to Warragul every day hourly from 7AM-midnight, excluding Sunday when the last train leaves at 6:25PM. Myki is used for most services, however a paper ticket may be needed -- check the V/Line website. Full timetable available here.
