
Banda Besar is an island in the Banda Islands.


Understand[edit]
Map of Banda Besar
It is the largest island in the Banda Islands.

Get in[edit]
Rent a small boat from Banda Neira.

Get around[edit]
You may rent a motorbike from local villagers to go around the island.

See[edit]
Fort Hollandia
-4.547937129.8811881 Fort Hollandia (Benteng Hollandia). Initially, Fort Hollandia was founded in 1642 under the name Fort Lonthoir. Later it was renamed Fort Hollandia by Pieter Vlak. Fort Hollandia was established with the aim of monitoring the movement of the Banda people, and monitoring sea traffic, especially the nutmeg trade in the Lonthoir and Neira sea lanes. (updated Oct 2022)

Do[edit]
Nutmeg and mace in Lonthoir
Strolling around the nutmeg plantations. Along the road in Banda Besar you will find nutmeg and mace that are drying in the sun.

Events[edit]
Cuci Parigi Pusaka, heirloom washing festival.
