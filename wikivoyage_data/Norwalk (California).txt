
Norwalk is a city in Los Angeles County in Southern California. It is 17 miles southeast of Downtown Los Angeles.


Get in[edit]
Map of Norwalk (California)


By car[edit]
Norwalk crosses the 5, 105, and 605 freeways and it's close to the 91 and 405 freeways.

By commuter rail[edit]
Norwalk is served by Metrolink commuter trains.

33.9139-118.1051 Norwalk railway station, 12901 Hoxie Ave.   (updated Mar 2022)
Get around[edit]
Norwalk Transit System[dead link] offers bus service in and around Norwalk. Fares are $1.25.
