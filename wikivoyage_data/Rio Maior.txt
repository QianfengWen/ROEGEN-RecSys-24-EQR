
Rio Maior is a city and municipality in Ribatejo, Portugal.


Understand[edit]
A park in Rio Maior.

Rio Maior (RREE-oo muh-YAWR, /ˈʁi.u ma.ˈjɔɾ/) municipality has 21,192 residents, with about 8,000 living in the city proper.

Get in[edit]
Rio Maior is on the A15 motorway (Autoestrada do Atlântico).
Regional bus service is provided by Rodoviária do Tejo.
