Liloan or Lilo-an is in Cebu Province, Philippines, a suburb located on the coast north of Cebu City.


Understand[edit]
This is a spread-out, suburban area.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Drive north from Mandaue taking one of the two main highways that connect Liloan with Mandaue; the Central Nautical Highway and the Cebu North Coast Road.

By bus[edit]
Buses to/from the North bus terminal in Mandaue and to/from Bogo using the "Hagnaya-North Terminal" bus route. Also bus and jeepney route 25 from North terminal.
Makes sure this is the bus north to Liloan (Metro Cebu), not one to Liloan Port in Santander at the extreme south of Cebu Island.

Get around[edit]
See Metro Cebu for general information about transport in the urban region.
