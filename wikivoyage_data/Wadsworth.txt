
Wadsworth is in Medina County, Ohio.


Understand[edit]
Wadsworth was founded in 1814 and was named after General Elijah Wadsworth, a hero of the Revolutionary War. The Wadsworth City School District is known for success in athletics, the arts, and academics.

Get in[edit]
Map of Wadsworth
By Car[edit]
Wadsworth is located just off of I-76 which runs from west of Akron east to New Jersey.
