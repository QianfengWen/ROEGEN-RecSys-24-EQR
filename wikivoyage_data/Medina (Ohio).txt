
Medina, pronounced muh-DYE-nuh is a city in Medina County, Ohio.


Get in[edit]
Map of Medina (Ohio)
Medina is located just off of I-71, which runs from Cleveland through Columbus to Cincinnati.  You can get to Medina from I-71's exit at State Route 3 northeast of Medina, or from I-71's exit at State Route 18 east of downtown Medina.
I-271 begins near Willoughby Hills, east of Cleveland and ends just Northeast of Medina.
State Route 3 begins in Cleveland and ends in Cincinnati.  SR 3 runs southwest into downtown Medina and Public Square, and then continues south through Medina County.
State Route 18 begins at the Indiana and Ohio border, near Hicksville and ends in Akron.  SR 18 runs east and west through downtown Medina and Public Square.
State Route 57 begins in Lorain and ends in Riceland.  SR 57 runs southeast through downtown Medina and Public Square.
US 42 begins in Cleveland and ends in Louisville, Kentucky.  US 42 runs southwest through downtown Medina and Public Square.
Get around[edit]
Medina County Public Transit provides limited bus service.
