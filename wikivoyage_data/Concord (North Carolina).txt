Concord is a city in North Carolina and fast-growing bedroom community of the major city of Charlotte. In 2020, the population was around 107,000,  making it the second largest city of the Charlotte Metro.

Map of Concord (North Carolina)

By plane[edit]
Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT IATA)
35.387778-80.7091671 Concord Regional Airport (USA  IATA). Allegiant Air flight from Fort Lauderdale, New Orleans, Orlando/Sanford, Punta Gorda and St. Petersburg/Clearwater   
By bus[edit]
The 80x bus operated by Charlotte Area Transit System (CATS)[dead link] offers service from Charlotte to Concord on weekdays. Geared towards commuters, it goes from Concord to Charlotte in the morning and from Charlotte to Concord in the late afternoon. This schedule makes it usable for going to Concord to spend a night or more, but not for a day trip.
Concord Kannapolis Area Transit offers service from Kannapolis.
