
Mansfield is a country town in Victoria, Australia. It is a two-hour drive from Melbourne.

Get in[edit]
The main and primary mode of transport into the town is via car by a two and a half hour drive up the Yarra Valley. From Melbourne, use the M3 Eastern Freeway east up til around Ringwood, where then head up B300 for about two hours until the B320 junction, and then continue on B320 until Mansfield.
