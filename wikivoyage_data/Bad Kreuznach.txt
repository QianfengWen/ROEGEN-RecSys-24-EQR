
Bad Kreuznach is a spa town on the river Nahe located in the Rhineland-Palatinate region (Rheinland-Pfalz) of Germany and is the center of the Nahe wine region.  With a lengthy history as a center for baths and spas, Bad Kreuznach is where the region's best restaurants and hotels are.


Get in[edit]
Map of Bad Kreuznach


By train[edit]
There are a number of regional lines providing connect to the town from Saarbrücken, Neunkirchen (Saarland),  Idar-Oberstein, Mainz, Kaiserslautern and Bingen. In the summer this is also the excursion train Nahetal-Express from Wiesbaden.

By car[edit]
From the A 60 just south of Bingen, take the B 41 toward Bad Sobernheim.

49.8457997.8465353 The Römerhalle, Hüffelsheimer Straße 11. of particular note. The museum, built on a former smallholding, exhibits artifacts from excavations carried out in Kreuznach and the surrounding area, where a Roman villa was also uncovered. Of particular interest are the 3rd century mosaic floors, including one depicting Oceanus, the sea god in his element, and one depicting gladiators in combat. A Roman heating system (hypocaust) has also been uncovered for display. From here, you can enjoy a stroll in the park with its tropical trees and ponds, and visit the castle park museum.
