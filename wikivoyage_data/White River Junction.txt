
White River Junction is a village on the Connecticut River in eastern Vermont. It is in the town of Hartford.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
White River Junction is located at the junction of Interstate 89 and Interstate 91.

By bus[edit]
Greyhound, Sykes Avenue. Phone: +1 802 295-3011. Service from Boston Logan Airport and South Station to White River Junction.  Several motels nearby, 15-minute walk to the center of White River Junction.
Dartmouth Coach, Hanover/Lebanon: +1 603 448-2800.  Service from Boston Logan Airport, Boston South Station and New York City to Hanover (Dartmouth College) and Lebanon, both within ten miles of White River Junction.
Advance Transit, +1 802 295-1824. Serves the Upper Valley towns including Wilder, Norwich, Hanover, NH and West Lebanon, NH, Lebanon, Enfield and Canaan. All routes are free.
By plane[edit]
Cape Air serves New Hampshire's Lebanon Municipal Airport, three miles from White River Junction.  Flights to Boston Logan Airport and New York White Plains Airport.

Eat[edit]
There's a well stocked supermarket just across the bridge in Hartford. They have a nice salad bar at $5 per pound, great for a picnic. There are a few diners on the other side of the river in West Lebanon, a 15-minute walk. Shyrl's Diner has an OK breakfast, but don't order more than two pancakes! They're huge.
