


Valley (Welsh: Y Fali) is a village on Anglesey.


Understand[edit]
RAF Valley is to the south of the village, is as  Royal Air Force station concerned with the training of fast jet pilots and was the base for air sea rescue.

Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
Flights from Cardiff to 53.255355-4.5338851 Anglesey Airport (VLY  IATA) ceased in 2022.

By train[edit]
53.281-4.5632 Valley station is the last station before Holyhead with connections to Wrexham, Shrewsbury, Birmingham International, Crewe and Cardiff.

By car[edit]
Valley is reach via the A55 Holyhead - Chester
