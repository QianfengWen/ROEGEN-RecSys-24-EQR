
Doylestown is a village in Wayne County in Northeast Ohio. The population was 2,799 at the 2000 census.


Get in[edit]
Map of Doylestown (Ohio)
Located on the northeastern most corner of Wayne County, Doylestown is centrally located between the region's major metro areas.

Route 21 - Runs along the eastern border of Doylestown and connects the town to Akron and Cleveland to the north and Massillon and Canton to the south.
Route 585 - Originates along the northern border of the town and connects to Wooster in the southwest.
