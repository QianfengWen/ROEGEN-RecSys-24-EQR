Oestrich-Winkel is in the Rheingau, Southern Hesse in Germany. It is a community consisting of the villages  Winkel, Oestrich, Mittelheim and Hallgarten. The village has a population of about 11,400.

Get in[edit]
By road[edit]
On the B42 between Rüdesheim and Wiesbaden.
49.998258.020711 Rheinfähre. Ferry across the Rhine from Ingelheim to Oestrich-Winkel. 

By train[edit]
Rail station "Oestrich-Winkel" in Mittelheim by the line: Frankfurt – Wiesbaden – Rüdesheim – Koblenz – Neuwied

By Bus[edit]
Bus line 171 from Wiesbaden Hbf to Rüdesheim

On foot[edit]
The Rheinsteig walking trail passes through Wiesbaden-Biebrich, Kiedrich, Kloster Eberbach near Eltville, Schloss Vollrads near Oestrich-Winkel, Monstera Marienthal near Geisenheim and Niederwalddenkmal near Rüdesheim.
