
Urbana is a city in Ohio. It is the county seat of Champaign County.


Get in[edit]
Map of Urbana (Ohio)
By car[edit]
Urbana lies on US Route 68.

By plane[edit]
40.1325-83.75361 Grimes Field. A small general aviation airport.   (updated Aug 2019)
By bike[edit]
The Simon Kenton Trail goes through West Liberty and Bellefontaine to the north, and to Springfield to the south.
