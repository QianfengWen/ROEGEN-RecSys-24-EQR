
Cadaval is a town in Portugal's Oeste region.


Understand[edit]
Serra de Montejunto in Cadaval municipality.

The town of Cadaval (kuh-duh-VAHL, /kɐ.dɐ.ˈvaɫ/) itself has about 3,000 residents, while the population of the entire municipality is 13,382 (2021 preliminary).

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Take the A8 motorway to exit 11, then follow signs to Cadaval via the N361 road.
From the A1 motorway, take exit 5 and follow the N366 road toward Caldas da Rainha/N115, taking N115-1 after the roundabout near Cercal.

By bus[edit]
Bus service is provided by:

Boa Viagem from Alenquer
Barraqueiro Oeste from Torres Vedras
Rodoviária do Tejo & Rodoviária do Oeste from the Oeste region
By train[edit]
The nearest train station is in Bombarral, 7 km (4.3 mi) away.

See[edit]
39.2442-9.10341 Igreja de Nossa Senhora da Conceição (Church of Our Lady of the Conception). The current mother church of Cadaval was built in the 19th century, but paintings, tiles, and an altarpiece date back as far as the 16th century.   (updated Jun 2021)
39.1781-9.05042 Real Fábrica do Gelo (Royal Ice Factory). A national monument since 1997, the Royal Ice Factory was built in the 18th century in the Montejunto mountains to satisfy the royal court's desire for cold beverages during the summer heat.   (updated Jun 2021)
39.187449-9.0555543 Serra de Montejunto. 666 m (2,185 ft) high mountain in a protected landscape shared with Alenquer. It's the highest natural viewpoint in Estremadura province.   (updated Aug 2021)
