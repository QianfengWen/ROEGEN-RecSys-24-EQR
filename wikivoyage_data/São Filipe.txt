
São Filipe is a city on the island of Fogo.


Get in[edit]
Map of São Filipe
See the Fogo section for details on flights and boats.
From the airport a taxi into town costs 500$. A shared Aluguer costs 100$.
From the port, a taxi is around 150$.
For other destinations around the island aluguers leave from near the market.

Do[edit]
There is said to be a somewhat decent beach by the harbor outside of town, where the current isn't quite as strong.
