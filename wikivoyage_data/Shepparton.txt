
Shepparton is a city in The Murray region of Victoria.


Get in[edit]
0°0′0″N 0°0′0″EMap of Shepparton
From Melbourne it's around 175 km – that is, two hours' drive, by using the M31 Hume Freeway and the M39 Goulburn Valley Freeway north.
You can also catch a train from Southern Cross Station, Melbourne which takes 2.5 hours

Get around[edit]
By taxi[edit]
The town is small and buses infrequent. Hiring a taxi may be your best option.

By bike[edit]
Shepparton streets are quiet and the town is small so hiring a bicycle and riding is an option. There are lovely riding paths along the rivers. Bikes available for hire from Aquamoves and the Big4 Caravan Park.

By bus[edit]
Most bus routes around town operate each hour during business hours on weekdays, with a more limited timetable on Saturday and no services on Sunday.. For routes and timetable see the Public Transport Victoria website.
