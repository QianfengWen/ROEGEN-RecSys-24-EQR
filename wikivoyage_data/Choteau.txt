
Choteau is a town in Central Montana. It is the county seat of Teton County. About 1,700 people live there.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
Choteau is at the intersection of U.S. highway 89 and U.S. 287.
The nearest commercial airport is in Great Falls.

Get around[edit]
Map of Choteau
You will need a personal vehicle, or bike, to get around Choteau.
When adventuring outside of town, be sure you plan for fuel and download maps. Wide open country is great to explore, just don't expect to always have service and amenities.
