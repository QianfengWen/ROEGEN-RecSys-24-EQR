
Powell is a small town in Northwest Wyoming. It is home to Northwest College, a public community college. There were 6,300 people living in Powell in 2020.


Understand[edit]
Powell began as a cattle ranching community and beef production remains an important part of the economy.

Get in[edit]
Powell is located on Hwy 14.
The nearest airport is Yellowstone Regional Airport in Cody.
