
Matosinhos is city and municipality in Douro Litoral, Portugal. It is a suburb of Porto.


Understand[edit]
A Igreja do Bom Jesus de Matosinhos.

The municipality of Matosinhos (muh-too-ZEE-nyoosh, /mɐ.tu.ˈzi.ɲuʃ/) has 175,478 residents (2011) living in four civil parishes (freguesias):

41.2-8.631 Custóias, Leça do Balio e Guifões.   (updated Feb 2021)
41.190218-8.6923292 Matosinhos e Leça da Palmeira.   (updated Feb 2021)
41.223889-8.6988893 Perafita, Lavra e Santa Cruz do Bispo.   (updated Feb 2021)
41.19-8.614 São Mamede de Infesta e Senhora da Hora.   (updated Feb 2021)
