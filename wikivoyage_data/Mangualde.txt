
Mangualde is a city and municipality in Beira Alta, Portugal.


Understand[edit]
Mangualde's artificial beach.

Mangualde (muhn-GWAHL-d(ih), /mɐ̃.ˈgwaɫ.dɨ/) municipality has 18,294 residents (2021 preliminary), with about 7,300 living in the city proper.

Get in[edit]
40.586586-7.7602531 Estação Ferroviária de Mangualde (Mangualde Train Station). PDF timetable. Mangualde is on the Linha da Beira Alta (Beira Alta railway line) and is served by intercity and regional trains from Lisbon, Coimbra, Guarda, and Covilhã.  (updated Aug 2021)
Rede Expressos provides intercity bus service.
Mangualde is served by the A25 motorway (Autoestrada das Beiras Litoral e Alta).
