
Mold (Welsh: Yr Wyddgrug) is a town in Flintshire.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
Mold is 25 miles (40 km) east of Chester and 7 miles south-west of Deeside and the A55 North Wales Expressway.
Closest rail station is Buckley.
Arriva buses has services from Chester.
