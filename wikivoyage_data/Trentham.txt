
Trentham is a small shire between Daylesford and Woodend in  Victoria. It is about an hour northwest of Melbourne, and like Daylesford, has many spas.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
There is no public transport available. By car, it takes about 1 hour and 10 minutes from the centre of Melbourne. Drive along the Calder Freeway, turn off at Woodend and take the signs towards Daylesford.
