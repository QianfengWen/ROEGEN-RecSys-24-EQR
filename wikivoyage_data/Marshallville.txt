
Marshallville is a village in Wayne County in Northeast Ohio. The population was 826 at the 2000 census. The village has a total area of 0.6 mi2, all land.


Get in[edit]
Map of Marshallville
Route 94 runs north to south through town; interchanging at 585 and continuing into Wadsworth, North Royalton, Parma, and Cleveland to the north, and interchanging in Dalton and continuing into Amish country to the south.
