
Odivelas is city in Greater Lisbon, Portugal.


Understand[edit]
Modern church architecture in Ramada, Odivelas.

Odivelas (oh-dee-VEH-luhsh, /o.di.ˈve.ɫɐʃ/) municipality has 144,549 inhabitants (2011).
