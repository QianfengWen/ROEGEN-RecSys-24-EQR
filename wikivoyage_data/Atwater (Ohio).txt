
Atwater is a township in southeast Portage County.


Get in[edit]
Map of Atwater (Ohio)

SR 244, which runs east and west across most of Ohio, and SR 183, which runs from Ravenna to Carroll County intersect in Atwater.
From Interstate 76, take the Edinburg exit, go northwest on SR 14, to SR 183 South.  
From SR 44 take SR 224 east in Randolph.

Sleep[edit]
There is one campground in Atwater, but no hotels or inns.  Nearby Alliance or Kent would be your best bet for lodging.  

 Whispering Pines Campground, 1085 Industry Rd, ☏ +1 330 947-3504.
