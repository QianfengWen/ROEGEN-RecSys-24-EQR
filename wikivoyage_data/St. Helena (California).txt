Get in[edit]
Map of St. Helena (California)


By plane[edit]
San Francisco International Airport (SFO IATA) in San Francisco.
By car[edit]
From San Francisco:

Highway 101 North over the Golden Gate Bridge, to Highway 37;
Highway 37 East towards Vallejo/Napa, to Highway 121;
Highway 121 North towards Napa to Highway 29 North to St. Helena.
Get around[edit]
St. Helena is a small town but the wineries are spread out. Driving is recommended but biking can also be fun.
Please do not Drink and Drive. Even though you may not feel like you are drinking, it only takes a few wine tastings to be over the legal limit. Be safe and designate a driver or hire a taxi or limo.

 Robert Louis Stevenson Museum (formerly Silverado Museum), 1490 Library Ln, ☏ +1 707 963-3757. W-Su noon-4PM. The largest collection of Robert Louis Stevenson's works outside of Scotland. Eight thousand manuscripts, photos, and letters.

Wine Tasting[edit]
38.489057-122.4481011 V. Sattui, 1111 White Ln, toll-free: +1-800-799-2337. Summer 9AM-6PM, winter 9AM-5PM, closed only on Christmas day. Tastings cost $5-$10. They also have a wonderful deli and picnic tables.   
38.5175-122.482 Charles Krug Winery, 2800 Main St, ☏ +1 707 967-2200, toll-free: +1-888-747-5784. 10:30AM-5PM. This winery is a national historical land mark.   
 Heitz Cellars, 436 St. Helena Hwy. (Hwy 29), ☏ +1 707 963-3542. 11AM-4:30PM. Enjoy outside seating with views of the vineyards and wine tasting of several vintages and varieties of wines. 
 Louis M. Martini Winery, 254 South St. Helena Hwy (Hwy 29), ☏ +1 707 963-2736. 11AM-5PM. Cabernet Sauvignon and Italian varietals. 
38.510158-122.4798863 Beringer, 2000 Main St, ☏ +1 707 963-4812. Established in 1873, Beringer is the oldest winery in Napa Valley. Enjoy the history by taking the Vintage Legacy Tour at 11AM ($40) which takes you through the winery and tunnels and includes wine tasting and barrel tasting. Wine tasting: $10 3-wine flight.   
 Whitehall Lane Winery, 1563 St. Helena Hwy (Hwy 29), toll-free: +1-800-963-9454. 11AM-5PM. This family owned winery specializes in Cabernets. Wine tasting: $12-25. 
Spas[edit]
 Health Spa Napa Valley, 1030 Main St, ☏ +1 707 967-8800. Relax, indulge and detox at this peaceful spa in St. Helena. Health spa goes beyond the standard spa treatments, offering private Yoga and Pilates classes as well as fitness assessments. Massage starting at $75, Ffcials Starting at $120, body treatments starting at $120, private yoga $85. 
 Meadowood Napa Valley, 900 Meadowood Ln, ☏ +1 707-967-1200. Indulge in a spa treatment and send your husband out for a game of golf. Meadowood offers spa treatments, croquest, golf, tennis, swimming and hiking. Treatments starting at $135. 
Buy[edit]
 Main Street Book Store, 1315 Main St., ☏ +1 707 963-1338. Main Street Book Store is a fantastic independent book store in the heart of St. Helena with a knowledgeable owner that can help you navigate the narrow bookshelves to find the perfect book for you. 
Eat[edit]
 Gott's Roadside, 933 Main St, ☏ +1 707 963-3486. Daily 10:30AM-9PM. Good, cheap food! American-style drive-in with 1950s decor and great burgers.

Sleep[edit]
 El Bonita Motel, 195 Main St, toll-free: +1-800-541-3285. This budget motel is a good way to save a few dollars while enjoying the wine country. Their rooms are basic but the pool and hot tub are a treat after a day of wine tasting. El Bonita would be ideal for those traveling with children, rent a "Homestead" room and enjoy the kitchenette. Nov - Mar: $99–190, Mar-May: $125–269, May-Nov: $145–289. 
 Harvest Inn, One Main St, toll-free: +1-800-950-8466. An English Tudor lodge with beautifully decorated rooms, serene gardens and a luxurious spa. Most rooms with fireplaces. $125–359 Off Season, $419–529 High Season. (updated Jun 2018)
 Hotel St. Helena, 1309 Main St, ☏ +1 707 963-4388. Reminiscent of old fashion boarding houses. Some rooms have private baths while others share a bathroom down the hall. Call for prices. 
 SouthBridge Napa Valley, 1020 Main St, ☏ +1 707-967-9400. Luxurious rooms with views. Starting at $225. 
 Meadowood Resort, 900 Meadowood Ln, toll-free: +1-800-458-8080. 85 Cottages, suites and lodges forming an exclusive get away with its own golf course and spa. Suites: $350-3,125 depending on season and size. 
 Vineyard Country Inn, 201 Main St, ☏ +1 707 963-1000. A beautiful estate home with cottages in the back, offering 21 suites, swimming pool, jacuzzi and breakfast. Rooms: $185-325. 
 Wine Country Inn, 1152 Lodi Ln, ☏ +1 707 963-7077. Perched on a knoll overlooking manicured vineyards, the Wine Country Inn is a romantic B&B offering 24 rooms and five luxury cottages. 
 Ink House, 1575 St. Helena Highway (Hwy 29). This 1884 historic landmark hotel combines Victorian elegance and modern facilities in the midst of the Napa Valley's world famous wine growing region. Seven guest bedrooms from $179 + tax. No restaurant, but gourmet breakfast included.
