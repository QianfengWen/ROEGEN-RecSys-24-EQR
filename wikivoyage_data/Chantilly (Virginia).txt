
Chantilly is a city in Fairfax County, Virginia near Dulles International Airport.  The Steven F. Udvar-Hazy Center of the National Air and Space Museum is the most popular attraction in Chantilly.

Steven F. Udvar-Hazy Center of the National Air and Space Museum

Get in[edit]
Map of Chantilly (Virginia)

The Dulles International Airport (IAD IATA) is close to the Air and Space Museum.  To get to the museum from the airport, take Fairfax Connector bus 983.

By bus[edit]
Fairfax Connector is the local bus service. A few useful routes: #650 connects to the Metro Orange Line at Vienna/Fairfax-GMU station, #901 connects to the Metro Silver Line at Herndon station, and #983 runs between the Udvar-Hazy Center (see below) and Dulles Airport.

See[edit]
38.911444-77.4441111 Steven F. Udvar-Hazy Center - National Air and Space Museum, 14390 Air and Space Museum Parkway, ☏ +1 202 357-2200. 10AM-5:30PM. Located near Dulles International Airport, this Smithsonian museum opened in 2003 to house many illustrious air/spacecraft that would not fit into the 1976 museum in the National Mall. The Udvar-Hazy collection includes the SR-71 "Blackbird" spy plane, the Concorde supersonic jet, the Enola Gay B-29, the Super Constellation, and the space shuttle Discovery. In addition, the museum contain an observation tower and an IMAX theater. The museum is free; there is a charge for the IMAX. Parking is available for $15/vehicle.Public transportation to the Udvar-Hazy Center from Washington DC is now relatively easy; take the Silver Line Metro train to Innovation Center, then catch the 983 Fairfax Connector bus to the Udvar-Hazy Center. Buses run every 30 min M-F and once an hour on weekends. The 983 bus also stops at Dulles Airport, so it can be used by visitors with a layover there.   
38.8645-77.36952 Ox Hill Battlefield Park (Chantilly Battlefield Park), 4134 West Ox Road, ☏ +1 703 324-8700. A 5-acre (2-ha) farmland on which a major Civil War battle took place   
38.8614-77.43063 Ellanor C. Lawrence Park. Ellanor C. Lawrence Park is a large area preserved for the multitude of wild animals and plants. The park has various historic buildings, such as a smokehouse, dairy complex, beehives and gardens. It also has multiple sport fields and hiking trails. It has meadow, ponds, and a forest.
