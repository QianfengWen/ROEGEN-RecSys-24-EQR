
Pontresina is a village in Graubünden. The village is close to the ski resorts around St. Moritz and very busy during the winter season.


Get in[edit]
Trains go at least hourly from St. Moritz, which can be reached from Chur. Travelling from Italy, there are a number of trains a day from Tirano to the 46.490659.896071 Pontresina railway station
