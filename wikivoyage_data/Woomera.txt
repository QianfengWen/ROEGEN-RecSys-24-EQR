
 Woomera is a small Outback town in South Australia, situated 483 kilometres (300 miles) north of Adelaide. It is a military owned town and testing site with a current population of about 300.
The town is open, and tourism is encouraged.  Although all property in the town is owned by the military, land is leased by commercial enterprises, and shops and accommodation are available.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
Woomera is 5-6 hours' drive from Adelaide and 4 hours' drive from Coober Pedy.  The town is 6km off the highway down a well-marked road that turns off at the tiny hamlet of Pimba, where Spud's Roadhouse is a prominent feature.

Get around[edit]
Woomera is small and is centred around Banool and Dewrang Avenues, with the Missile Park at the intersection of these.
Petrol costs around 30c/litre more than on the coast.

Do[edit]
During the summer months from October to Anzac Day (25 April), the Woomera Swimming Pool complex is popular with locals and tourists. The complex features a 25-metre pool and a 1-metre diving board.

 Woomera Theatre, Dewrang Avenue. Shows films every most fortnight on Saturday evenings. It also has a photogenic entrance at other times. (updated Apr 2015)
 Bowling alley, Dewrang Avenue (in the Visitors Centre), ☏ +61 8 8673 7042. 6:30AM-3PM Weekday 7AM-3PM Weekends. Bowling Alley built back in the 80's by the Americans. Bookings required 24 hours in advance. (updated Apr 2019)
Buy[edit]
 Woomera Village Store, Dewrang Avenue (opposite Visitors Centre). Sells groceries and booze. (updated Apr 2015)
 Thrift Store, Dewrang Avenue. Saturday 10am. Great thrift shop full of lots of bargains - excellent to update your clothes whilst travelling! (updated Apr 2015)
Eat[edit]
 Eldo Hotel, Kotara Crescent (follow the signs). Standard pub food at standard outback prices (good drink prices though, especially during happy hour). Balcony overlooking the desert. Dinner 6pm-8:30pm, bar open later (from 9:30pm on Mondays to 12:30am on weekends). $20-$35. (updated Apr 2015)
 Spud's Roadhouse, Pimba, where the turnoff from the highway is. Typical roadhouse fare, including lavish burgers. $10-$30. (updated Apr 2015)
