Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By train[edit]
Merzig has two rail stations on the Saarstrecke that runs between Saarbrücken and Trier.

By bus[edit]
SaarLuxBus [dead link] links Merzig with Luxembourg City every hour, taking 1:20 hours.

By car[edit]
Merzig is just off the A8 about 45 km northwest of Saarbrücken.
