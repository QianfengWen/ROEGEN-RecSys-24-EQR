
Eminence [dead link] is in the Ozarks and the southeast region of Missouri.

Downtown

Understand[edit]
Eminence is a city in Shannon County, Missouri, United States.  Known as Missouri's Outdoor Playground, it is located along the Jacks Fork River and is near the world famous Current River.

Get in[edit]
Eminence is off the beaten path.  Two highways pass through town, State Highway 19 and Highway 106.  Most visitors arrive from Salem, Missouri north of Eminence via Highway 19 or turn off on Highway 19 from U.S. Highway 60 at Winona, Missouri.
