
Blaenavon (Welsh: Blaenafon) is a town in Torfaen, South Wales.  The Blaenavon Industrial Landscape has been named a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Blaenavon from the grounds of the Big Pit museum

Get in[edit]
Just south of the A465 Heads of the Valleys Road on the B4246 7 miles of Abergavenny.
bus 34 from Brynmawr or X24 from Newport.
