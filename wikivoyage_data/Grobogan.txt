
Grobogan is a regency in Central Java. Its main draw for visitors is Bledug Kuwu, a mud volcano that erupts salty mud at regular intervals.


Understand[edit]
Orientation[edit]
Grobogan is located in the northeastern part of the Central Java province in Indonesia. It's a flat valley lying between two mountains, Pegunungan Kendeng to the south and Pegunungan Kapur Utara to the north. Its capital is the town of Purwodadi. Most people are Javanese.

Eat[edit]
Gambringan Railway Station
Pecel Gambringan is a specialty of Grobogan pecel (Javanese salad in peanut sauce). The typical veggies of this dish include papaya leaf, kecipir (winged bean), and kembang turi (the flower of Sesbania grandiflora). It is served on a banana leaf plate called pincuk. In the past, the food was offered in the Gambringan Railway Station, with some women carrying the ingredient on their backs, walking from coach to coach when the train stopped. But now, this food can be easily found outside the station.

-7.144063110.9151881 Pecel Gambringan Mbak Yayuk, Pucang Kidul, Tambirejo, Toroh. 08:00-17:00. Serves pecel Gambringan. Also, some side dishes like peyek (Indonesian cracker) and gorengan (fritter). From Rp5,000. (updated Sep 2022)
Another special food in Grobogan is garang asem, processed chicken dishes cooked using banana leaves and dominated by sour and spicy flavors.

-7.075563110.9164382 RM Raharjo, Jl. Jendral Sudirman, Purwodadi, ☏ +62 292 424287. 07:00-18:00. Serves garang asem using ayam kampung (free-range chicken). From Rp25,000. (updated Sep 2022)
Becek kerbau, water buffalo soup is a also must try with the freshly sour taste because of using daun dayakan and daun kedondong (June plum leaves).

-7.070937110.9248133 Waroeng Sedep Yanto Ganjar, Jalan Purwodadi Blora KM. 02 Getasrejo, ☏ +62 812 281 74744. 08:30-21:00. Serves becek kerbau. From Rp30,000. (updated Oct 2022)
For vegetarian, you may eat sego pager for breakfast in Godong District. The dish is rice with vegetables like papaya leaves, cassava leaves, bean sprouts, and others doused with peanut sauce and sprinkled with uyah goreng (fried salty grated coconut). 

-7.024938110.7711874 Sego Pager Parjiah, Jalan Pemuda, Godong, ☏ +62 857 0223 1292. 05:30-09:00. Serves sego pager. Around Rp10,000. (updated Oct 2022)
