Sisimiut [dead link] (Danish: Holsteinsborg) is a town in Western Greenland.


Get in[edit]
Sisimiut has a small airport to where Air Greenland operates flights from Kangerlussuaq and Nuuk daily, several times a week also from Ilulissat, Maniitsoq and Aasiaat.
There is a very simple road for 4x4 vehicles from Kangerlussuaq to Sisimiut.

Get around[edit]
66°52′34″N 53°35′11″WMap of Sisimiut
Since the distances are very small, everything is walkable. However, a bus runs a loop through the town every 20 minutes.
The airport is reachable by taxi.
