
Alcoutim is a town and municipality in Portugal's Algarve region, across from Spain on the Guadiana River.


Understand[edit]
Roman ruins at Villa Romana do Montinho das Laranjeiras.

Alcoutim (ahl-koo-TEEM , /aɫ.ko.ˈtĩ/) municipality has 2,521 residents (2012 preliminary). It is one of the three landlocked municipalities in the Algarve.

Get in[edit]
Eva Transportes offers regional bus service, with route 34 proving a connection from Vila Real de Santo António.
The nearest motorway is the A22 (Via do Infante de Sagres). From exit 18, follow IC27 or N122 to N122-1 into town.
