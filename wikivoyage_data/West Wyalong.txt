
West Wyalong is in New South Wales.

Main Street (Newell Highway) at West Wyalong

Understand[edit]
West Wyalong is the largest cereal growing district in the centre of New South Wales. Owing to its location on the Newell Highway, it makes it a popular stop over stop over spot for truckies.

Get in[edit]
West Wyalong is on the Newell Highway, 2 hrs from Dubbo, 6 from Melbourne, 4 from Sydney and 10 from Brisbane.
