

View of downtown Bucyrus on South Sandusky Avenue.
Bucyrus, nicknamed America's Bratwurst Capital, is a city in Crawford County, in Ohio.


Get in[edit]
Map of Bucyrus (Ohio)
By car[edit]
Bucyrus is connected by US30 leading west to Upper Sandusky, and east to Mansfield.

By plane[edit]
 Cleveland Hopkins International Airport (CLE IATA), ☏ +1 216-265-6030. Cleveland Hopkins International Airport is Cleveland's main airport, located on the west side of the city. The airport is served by most of the major domestic airlines, and it is a hub for United Airlines as well. 
 John Glenn Columbus International Airport (CMH IATA), 4600 International Gateway, Columbus, ☏ +1 614-239-4083. Direct flights to most major American cities.
