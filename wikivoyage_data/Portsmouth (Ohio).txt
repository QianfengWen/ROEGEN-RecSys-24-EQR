
Portsmouth[dead link] is a city in Southwest Ohio, it is the county seat of Scioto County. Portsmouth is full of history so take time to visit the antique shops, cafes and pubs in the Bonneyfiddle District. One of the main attractions in Portsmouth is the 2,200-foot floodwall mural painted by famed artist Robert Dafford. It is truly amazing and unique.


Get in[edit]
Map of Portsmouth (Ohio)
38.840556-82.8472221 Greater Portsmouth Regional Airport (Located in nearby Minford).   (updated Dec 2018)
By car, Portsmouth can be accessed using U.S. Route 23 (North/South) and U.S. Route 52 (East/West). 
Greyhound serves Portsmouth by bus.
Travelling by own boat is quite popular along the Ohio river and nearby Scioto river is popular for canoeing.
Amtrak serves the city via its thrice-weekly Cardinal train, running between Washington, D.C. and Chicago. Trains call in the small town of South Shore, Kentucky, just across the Ohio river.

38.7212-82.96372 South Portsmouth–South Shore station, Main Street & US 23. There's no public transport available at the station so make sure to call a taxi or arrange private transport beforehand.   (updated Jan 2022)
Get around[edit]
Other than a couple cab companies, there are no forms of public transit.  Although, a traveler can easily walk to many locations downtown.
