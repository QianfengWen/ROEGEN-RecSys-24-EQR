
Aberffraw (Welsh: Aberffro) is a village on Anglesey.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
On the A 4080, 11 miles from Valley (Anglesey)
Bus numbers 25 and 42 from Holyhead and Bangor. See the county council's web site for timetables.
It is on the Anglesey Coastal Path
