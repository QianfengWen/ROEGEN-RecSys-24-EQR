
Ingonish, Ingonish Beach, Ingonish Ferry, Ingonish Centre and South Ingonish Harbour are five small tourist settlements on the northwestern corner of Cape Breton Island in Nova Scotia, next to Cape Breton Highlands National Park.
The town is on the Cabot Trail, a scenic highway on Cape Breton Island.

46.6461-60.40171 Ingonish Visitor Centre, cnr Cabot Trail and Park Headquarters Lane. A visitor centre that opened its doors in 2014 that has information about the nearby Cape Breton Highlands National Park. (updated Apr 2023)
Get in[edit]
Ingonish is 110 km from North Sydney, and 74 km from the Trans-Canada Highway. From the junction between Highways 30 and 105 near St Anns, continue up north for 74 km until you're at Ingonish.
