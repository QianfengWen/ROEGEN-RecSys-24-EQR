
Vermilion is a town of 4,000 (2016) in East Central Alberta, Canada.


Understand[edit]
Main Street, Vermilion
Vermilion is a town in east central Alberta within the County of Vermilion River. It is a good pit stop for travellers along the Yellowhead Highway.

Get in[edit]
Map of Vermilion (Alberta)
By car[edit]
Vermilion is located at the intersection of Highway 16 (Yellowhead Highway) and Highway 41 (Buffalo Trail), approximately 60 km (37 mi) west of Lloydminster and 192 km (119 mi) east of Edmonton.

By bus[edit]
 Rider Express, toll-free: +1-833-583-3636. Operates a route between Edmonton and Regina with stops including in Vegreville, Vermilion, Lloydminster, North Battleford, Saskatoon, and Davidson. (updated Apr 2022)
