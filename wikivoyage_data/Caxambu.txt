

Caxambu
Caxambu is in Southern Minas Gerais.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
From Rio de Janeiro - via BR-116 (Presidente Dutra) west past Resende to the village of Engenheiro Passos and take BR-354 north.
From Belo Horizonte or São Paulo - via BR-381 to Campanha, then west on BR-267

 Lysandro Guimarães Water Park (Parque das Águas Lysandro Guimarães), Rua João Carlos, 100 - Centro, ☏ +55 35 3341-3266. Reputed to be the only place in the world with 12 mineral water springs, each with different chemical properties.
