
Cranbrook is a town in Kent.

Union Windmill

Understand[edit]
Cranbrook is a small town in the Weald of Kent. Cranbrook has been a market town since 1289, and has a population of just over 7000.

Get in[edit]
Cranbrook is just off the A229 road between Maidstone and Hastings.
Arriva bus route 5 runs from Maidstone and Staplehurst (the nearest railway station).
