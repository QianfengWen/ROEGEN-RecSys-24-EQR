
Bridgewater is a city in Massachusetts. Its population was about 29,000 in the 2020 census, and it is home to Bridgewater State College. This article also covers East Bridgewater, population about 14k in the 2020 census; and West Bridgewater, population about 8k in the 2020 census.


Get in[edit]
Map of Bridgewater (Massachusetts)

Bridgewater is centrally located at the interchange of Interstate 495 and Route 24. Routes 104, and 18 intersect at the town's center. East Bridgewater is directly on Route 18.

By train[edit]
41.9853-70.96581 Bridgewater Station, 85 Burrill Ave. Accessible boarding for all train cars. 499 parking spaces, 10 accessible. Bike racks. MBTA zone 6. $10.50 to Boston; parking: M-F $4, Sa-Su $2.   (updated Apr 2022)
Get around[edit]
See[edit]
41.988622-70.9660061 Bridgewater State University, 31 Summer St, ☏ +1 508-531-1000. A 235-acre college campus right in the center of town, it was founded in 1840 by Horace Mann as one of America's first teacher preparation institutions. BSC sports teams (the Bears) provide sports fans with games to watch.   (updated Dec 2020)
42.0141-71.01562 Reverend James Keith Parsonage (Keith House), 199 River St (West Bridgewater), ☏ +1 508-559-1510. By appointment. Built in approximately 1662, this is perhaps the oldest remaining parsonage in the United States. Today it operates as a museum and is owned by the Old Bridgewater Historical Society.   (updated Apr 2022)
42.0017-70.90333 Robbins Pond (East Bridgewater). Largest body of water in town.   (updated Apr 2022)

Eat[edit]
Town Green and Grace Bible Church in East Bridgewater
 Asack's Turkey Farm and Dairy Bar, 235 N Main Street, ☏ +1 508 580-7711. 11AM-9PM. You can walk out with a turkey pocket, turkey pot pie or turkey hot dog. The dairy bar is open year round and the portions are generous. It is a great place to grab a cold treat on a hot summer night. $5-10. (updated Mar 2022)
 The Charlie Horse, 647 Old West Center St. This is a casual bar-and-grill style restaurant whose menu features burgers, chicken dishes, salads, and fried seafood options. Prices range from app. $3 for appetizers to app. $16 for some dishes. The Charlie Horse also features an arcade game room, billiard tables, and central bar outfitted with televisions for watching sporting events. (updated Mar 2022)
 Crispi's Italian Cuisine, 136 Broad Street, ☏ +1 508 697-6733. 
 Daybreak Family Restaurant, 337 W Center St, ☏ +1 508 583-5341. 5AM - 2PM. A popular weekend spot for a fast delicious breakfast. Breakfast is served all day and there is a wide range of items to choose from. The most popular choices include eggs benedict and breakfast sandwiches. Although the restaurant is pretty small, they keep customers moving in and out so the waiting time is usually under 15 minutes. $4-12. (updated Mar 2022)
 Emma's Pub and Pizza, 1420 Pleasant Street (Route 104), ☏ +1 508 697-8815. 11AM-10PM. Drink specials and a large menu of reasonably priced appetizers and entrees keep Emma's busy Monday through Friday. There is a spacious outdoor deck, 40-seat bar, and large dining area. There most popular item is the 99-cent cheese pizza which is served every Wednesday from 8:30 to 10:30PM. The atmosphere Emma's provides is suitable for all. $10-30. (updated Mar 2022)
