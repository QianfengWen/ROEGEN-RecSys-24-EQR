Get around[edit]
Map of Hamilton (Victoria)
See[edit]
-37.741833142.0361011 Sir Reginald Ansett Transport Museum, Ballarat Rd &, Riley St, ☏ +61 3 5571 2767. Daily 10AM-4PM. A museum dedicated to Ansett Australia, including the display of the airline's first aircraft (Fokker Universal), models, vehicles and other memorabilia. Adults $10, children/seniors $8, family $20. (updated May 2020)
Do[edit]
Hamilton Botanic Gardens. Includes rare plants, a restored rotunda, and a small zoo.
The Keeping Place. A small museum and living history centre run by local indigenous people.
