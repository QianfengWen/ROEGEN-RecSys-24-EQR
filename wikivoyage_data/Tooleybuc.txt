
Tooleybuc is in the western Riverina region of New South Wales.


Understand[edit]
Tooleybuc is a small bordertown with a population of about 200 on the New South Wales side of the Murray River.
Tooleybuc is in the Murray River Council Local Government Area, with Moama as its main town.

 Echuca-Moama Visitors Information Centre, 2 Heygarth Street, Echuca (near the roundabout on the Cobb Highway/B75), ☏ +61 3 5480 7555, toll-free: +61 1800 804 446, info@echucamoama.com. M-F 9AM-5PM. 
Get in[edit]
35°1′40″S 143°20′18″EMap of Tooleybuc
The only way to get to Tooleybuc is via car via Yanga Way from NSW or via B400/B12 from Victoria.

Get around[edit]
Everything in Tooleybuc is within walking distance.
