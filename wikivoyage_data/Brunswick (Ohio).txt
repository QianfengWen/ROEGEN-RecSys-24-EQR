
Brunswick is the largest city in Medina County, Ohio


Get in[edit]
41°14′46″N 81°49′23″WMap of Brunswick (Ohio)
By car[edit]
I-71 runs North and South through the middle of Brunswick, with an exit at State Route 303.  I -71 begins in Cleveland and ends in Louisville, Kentucky.
SR 303 runs East and West through the middle of Brunswick.  SR 303 begins in Wakeman and ends near Windham.
US 42 begins in Cleveland and ends in Louisville, Kentucky.  US 42 somewhat parallels I-71.  US 42 runs Southwest through Brunswick.
Get around[edit]
You'll most likely have to get around by car, but some bus stations exist. Other than that, you can only walk within the city.
