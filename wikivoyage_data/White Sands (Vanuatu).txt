
White Sands is on the island of Tanna in Vanuatu. It is part of Vanuatu's Tafea District.

19°30′12″S 169°26′48″EMap of White Sands (Vanuatu)

Understand[edit]
The White Sands region is one of the most populated regions on the east coast of Tanna. It actually includes several small villages, like Namakara and Sulphur Bay.
These villages are very close to an active volcano, Mount Yasur.

Get in[edit]
A road goes from Lenakel across the island. If you follow this road, you should eventually come to the village of White Sands.

Get around[edit]
The roads connecting the villages at White Sands are primitive dirt roads. Getting around the region is probably much easier by foot than by car.

Go next[edit]
Not far from White Sands in the village of Port Resolution. Also known as Ireupuow, the village is near a restaurant which is made entirely of beach huts, and it actually located on the beach.
There is a village called White Grass, which has Tanna's only airport.
Lenakel is a beach town located on the other side of Tanna. It has some restaurants and guest houses where people can stay.
