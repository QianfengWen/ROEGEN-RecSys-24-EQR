Gawler is a city about 40 km north of Adelaide, in Adelaide Region.


Understand[edit]
Gawler has a population of about 26,000. During the colonial years, Gawler served as a resting stop between Adelaide and the copper mines at Kapunda and Burra.

Get in[edit]
By air[edit]
Gawler is served primarily by Gawler Airport.
Adelaide Airport is about 40 km south of Gawler in Adelaide.
By train[edit]
Gawler is well-served by commuter trains from Adelaide, with departures every 15 minutes during peak times. There are several railway stations in Gawler, with Gawler Central being closest to city centre.
