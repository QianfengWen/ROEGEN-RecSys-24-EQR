
Bethel is a town in Oxford County (Maine). It is near the White Mountain National Forest and home to Sunday River ski resort. Outward Bound and Gould Academy are also here.


Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Interstate 95 (Maine Turnpike) to Exit 63 "Gray" Route 26. Take Route 26 North.

Get around[edit]
The Mountain Explorer[dead link] - a free shuttle providing shuttle service between Bethel Village and the Sunday River Ski Resort.

Cafés & bakeries[edit]
 Café DiCocoa and Dicocoa's Market Bakery, 119 and 125 Main St, ☏ +1 207 824-5282, dicocoa@cafedicocoa.com. A cozy café-restaurant serving up pastries and a wide selection of coffee and hot drinks throughout the day, and then global cuisine at night. The market, which is next door, is filled with groceries, coffee and wine from around the globe. 
 The Good Food Store, 212 Mayville Rd, ☏ +1 207 824-3754, GoodFood@goodfoodbethel.com. Great selection of natural foods and drinks, along with a made-to-order sandwich service. Fine wines and cheeses, specialty beers, great soups, heat and eat meals, organic and local produce. The area's best catering.
