
Bridgewater is a town in South Shore region of Nova Scotia, at the navigable limit of the LaHave River. With a population of 8,790 (2021), Bridgewater is the largest town in the area.


Understand[edit]
Map of Bridgewater (Nova Scotia)
Priding itself as "The Main Street of the South Shore," Bridgewater has long been established as the primary commercial and professional service centre in the southern half of the province. The community boasts a diverse local economy, as well as larger national and international employers.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Bridgewater is about 100 km (62 mi) southwest of Halifax on Highway 103, take exits 12 or 13.

By bus[edit]
 Maritime Bus, toll-free: +1-800-575-1807, info@maritimebus.com. Operates an inter-regional bus service between destinations in Nova Scotia, New Brunswick, and Prince Edward Island. Its route between Halifax and Lunenburg includes a stop in Bridgewater. (updated Jan 2023)
