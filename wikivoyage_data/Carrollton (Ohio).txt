Carrollton is a village in Carroll County, Ohio.  It is the county seat.


Get in[edit]
Map of Carrollton (Ohio)
By car[edit]
Located at intersection of RT 43, midway between Canton and Steubenville, and RT 39, midway between New Philadelphia and East Liverpool. No mass transit.

By plane[edit]
Carroll County–Tolson Airport is a general aviation facility 1 mile southeast of the village at Airport Road off Rt 43 South of the village.
