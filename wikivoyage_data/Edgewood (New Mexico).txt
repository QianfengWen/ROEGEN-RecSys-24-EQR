
Edgewood is a town in Central New Mexico, located east of Albuquerque on the other side of the Sandia Mountains.


Get in[edit]
Edgewood lies on Interstate Highway 40 about 20 miles east of Albuquerque.
The North Central Regional Transit District "Blue Buses" provides free bus service to Santa Fe (with stops in Moriarty, Stanley and Galisteo) Monday through Friday with routes that extend to connect the counties and communities of Santa Fe, Taos, Los Alamos and Rio Arriba.
