
Curitibanos is in the Serrana region of Santa Catarina.


Understand[edit]
The municipality is a major agricultural producer, with emphasis on the production of cereals such as soybeans and corn, fruit growing, especially apples, persimmons and peaches. Also noteworthy is the production of garlic, which has already given it the title of "national capital of garlic"
Cattle raising stands out.


Get in[edit]
BR-116 passes by near the eastern end of town, connecting from Lages and eventually Porto Alegre in the south, or from Santa Cecília and eventually Curitiba in the north
BR-470 connects from Campos Novos in the west or Blumenau in the east
