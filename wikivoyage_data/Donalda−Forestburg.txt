
Donalda and Forestburg are two villages about 40 minutes apart in a rural area of East Central Alberta.


Understand[edit]
Map of Donalda−Forestburg
52.5828-112.5721 Donalda.    (population 225) is a village is home to the "World's Largest Oil Lamp", standing at 12.8 metres (42 ft) high and is one of the Giants of the Prairies. 52.5803-112.0682 Forestburg.   , (population 800) is an agricultural and coal mining community that's home to a small railway. 

Get in[edit]
Donalda is located just east of the Highway 53/56 junction, 40 km (25 mi) northeast of Stettler, 80 km (50 mi) east of Ponoka, and 165 km (103 mi) southeast of Edmonton. Forestburg is about 35 km (22 mi) east of Donalda via Highway 53 and 16 km (9.9 mi) west of Highway 36.

Get around[edit]
Being in rural Alberta, a private vehicle is the only realistic way to get around.
