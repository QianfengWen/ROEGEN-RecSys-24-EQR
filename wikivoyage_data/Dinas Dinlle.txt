
Dinas Dinlle is a small village on the coast of Gwynedd south of Caernarfon.


Understand[edit]
Blue flag quality beach (sand and stone) in Caernarfon bay. On a clear day providing great views of the Welsh hills.

Get in[edit]
7 miles (11 km) from Caernarfon via A487 and A499.
Bus 91[dead link] from Caernarfon.
