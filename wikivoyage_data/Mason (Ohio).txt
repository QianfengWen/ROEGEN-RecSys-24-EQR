Get in[edit]

By car[edit]
From Interstate I-71 northbound, take Ohio Exit 24 (Western Row Road/Kings Island).  At the end of the exit ramp, turn left (West) on Western Row Road/Tylersville Road.  Stay right on the three lane road to stay on Tylersville Road.  At the intersection of Tylersville Road with Mason-Montgomer Road, turn right (north).  One mile north, Mason-Montgomery Road intersects with Main Street (State Route 42).  You are now in downtown Mason, Ohio.
From Interstate I-71 southbound, take the Kings Mills Road exit.  Kings Mills Road west.  At a T-intersection, Route 741 turns north, Kings Mills turns south.  Kings Mills Road curves through a residential neighborhood before turning back to the west.  Kings Mills Ts into Main Street (State Route 42).  Turn left (west) on Main Street about 3 blocks to the intersection of Main Street with Mason-Montgomery Road.  You are in downtown Mason, Ohio.
From Interstate I-75, take the Tylersville Road exit.  Drive east to the intersection of Mason-Montgomery Road with Tylersville Road.  Turn left (north) onto Mason-Montgomery Road.  One mile north, Mason-Montgomery Road intersects with Main Street (State Route 42).  You are now in downtown Mason, Ohio.

By bus[edit]
SORTA, Southwest Ohio Regional Transit Authority, provides bus service from downtown Cincinnati to a parking lot at Western Row Road and Columbia Road and along Bus Route 71/72 (I-71/Columbia Road/Montgomery Road/Fields Ertel Road).

By plane[edit]
Mason served by three major airports:

John Glenn Columbus International Airport, Columbus (CMH IATA).
Cincinnati-Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG IATA) [ http://www.cvgairport.com/].
James M Cox Dayton International Airport, Dayton[dead link] (DAY IATA).
Each airport can be accessed from Mason with at least some portion of interstate driving.
Estimated driving time from Mason to the airports are:

Mason to CVG: 45 minutes; I-71 South to I-75 South to I-275 West to Airport Exit.
Mason to CMH: 90 minutes; I-71 North to I-670 East to Airport Exit, Exit #9.
Mason to DAY: 60 minutes; Tylersville Road to I-75 North, through downtown Dayton, to I-70 West to Airport Exit.

Do[edit]
Kings Island
39.3413-84.27251 Kings Island, 6300 Kings Island Dr, Kings Island, ☏ +1 513-754-5700. Kings Island is one of the world's greatest amusement parks. The park is divided into three different areas - the main park with adult attractions, a children's park, and a water park. Admission to the park grants access to every one of the areas. Kings Island is also the home to record holding coasters like the Beast, the world's longest wooden roller coaster.   (updated Nov 2023)
39.3617-84.26212 The Beach Waterpark (The Beach at Adventure Landing), 2590 Waterpark Dr, ☏ +1-513-398-7946, toll-free: +1-800-886-SWIM (7946). At The Beach Waterpark, parents and kids get soaked at more than 40 water rides and attractions. Go airborne on the five-story drop of The Cliff, ride the foaming river of Volcanic Panic, and propel your body at 65-miles per hour on The Banzai. With volleyball and basketball courts, teen and reggae dance nights, and the award-winning Dive-In Movies, a visit will truly be a day at The Beach!   
39.3509-84.2613 Great Wolf Lodge (Great Wolf Lodge - Mason’s Water Park), 2501 Great Wolf Dr, toll-free: +1-800-913-9653. With over 400 lodge-themed suites you can get soaked at one of America's largest indoor water parks, measuring 78,000 sq. ft., with eleven waterslides, six pools, an interactive treehouse waterfort, and 410,000 gallons of heated water - all year round. 
 The Golf Center at Kings Island, 6042 Fairway Drive, ☏ +1 513 398-7700. 18 Holes of Golf designed by Jack Nicklaus. Experience the Grizzly-the 18 hole championship course played by the best of the PGA, the LPGA, and the Senior PGA TOUR for the last 30 years. 
Professional sports[edit]
 Western & Southern Financial Group Masters & Women's Open. The best and brightest in tennis visit Mason every year for this premier tour stop. The Women's event is held in July and features players the likes of Serena Williams. The Men's event is held in August and features players the likes of Roger Federer and Rafael Nadal. 
 AVP Cincinnati Open: Pro Beach Volleyball Championship Series. The best men's and women's pro beach volleyball players battle it out in the AVP's season-ending event in late August. Mason will host the tournament through 2011.
