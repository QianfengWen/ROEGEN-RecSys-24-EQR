
Yeongcheon is in North Gyeongsang. Between Seoul and Busan on the Gyeongbu Expressway, the city is the birthplace of Choe Mu-seon and Jeong Mong-ju.

Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
The closest major airport is 35.894167128.6588891 Daegu International Airport (TAE IATA), about 42 km southwest of Yeongcheon.

By train[edit]
35.9595128.9392 Yeongcheon station. Yeongcheon's railway station is on the Jungang and Daegu Lines.   
By car[edit]
From Seoul, drive along the Gyeongbu Expressway for around 350 km.

Temples[edit]
35.9921128.791 Eunhaesa Temple, 479 Chiil-ri, Cheongtong-myeon (catch a bus from Yeongcheon bus station), ☏ +82 54-335-3318. Literally meaning "Temple of the Silver Sea", the interior is as beautiful as its name. Eunhaesa was built in 809 during the reign of the Silla Kingdom. ₩3,000 entrance fee.   (updated Sep 2018)

Buy[edit]
 Yeongcheon Public Market, 982-3 Wansan-dong (10 minute walk from Seodaemun Prison), ☏ +82 54-331-1772. A traditional Korean-style market selling fresh food, medicinal herbs, groceries as well as local arts and crafts. It is primarily for the locals so it is a challenge for English speakers to get by. Try the twisted bread sticks and gomtang soup. (updated Sep 2018)
