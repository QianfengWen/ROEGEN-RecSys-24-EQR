
Yarrawonga-Mulwala is a twin city on the border of the Murray River in the Riverina.


Get in[edit]
The quickest way to get to Yarrawonga-Mulwala is via car. While there are trains, they're infrequent and not a reliable way of getting here. But from Albury, it's a 1.5 hour drive west via the B400 Murray Valley Highway and from Melbourne it's a 4 hour drive via the M31 Hume Fwy and the C373 Benalla-Yarrawonga Rd.
