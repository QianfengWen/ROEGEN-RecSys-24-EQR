
Burren Junction is a hamlet of 225 (2021) in Northwestern New South Wales. It is in the Walgett Shire Local Government Area.


Understand[edit]
Burren Junction started as a railway encampment in 1902.

Visitors information centre[edit]
Event Guide
The nearest Visitor Information Centre is in Walgett.
Get in[edit]
Although there is a railway line, you will only be travelling on it if you're a shipment of wheat. There is an airstrip at Burren Junction Airport, but no scheduled flights. 

By road[edit]
On the Kamilaroi Highway (B51)
