
Castle Donington is a village in north Leicestershire on the border with Derbyshire.


Understand[edit]
The village is small, and the area is dominated by the airport and motor racing track.

Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
52.826572-1.3320211 East Midlands airport (EMA  IATA). South on a hill above the village. The airport mainly supports low-cost airlines such as Jet2.com, Ryanair and tour operators like Thomson Airways which serve a range of domestic and European short-haul destinations.   

By car[edit]
Exit at junction 24 of the M1 just north of the A 42. The village is the north west route off the traffic island while the airport is south west. The village is about 10 miles south of Derby via the A6/A50 and 11 miles north of Loughborough via the A6.

By rail[edit]
The closest rail station East Midlands Parkway but this is in the middle of nowhere with no local bus connections. Using one of the stations served by the Skylink service may be more convenient.

By bus[edit]
The village, Donington Park and the airport are served by a number of bus services.

 Skylink (Trent-Barton). Nottingham - Long Eaton - Castle Donington - Loughborough 
 Skylink (Kinchbus). Derby - Castle Donington - Loughborough - Leicester 
 Coalville Airlink (Roberts Coaches). Coalville - Castle Donington
