
Mörfelden-Walldorf  is a small town in Hesse.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
On the B486 between Rüsselsheim and Langen south of Frankfurt airport.
The S8 provides a rail connection to Frankfurt main station.

Get around[edit]
Map of Mörfelden
See[edit]

 Timber framed houses (Fachwerkhäuser). There are a number of old house in the town such as the Alte Schmiede built in 1693 in the Hintergasse. (updated Feb 2016)
 Mörfelder Heimatmuseum (Local history museum), Langgasse 45. (updated Feb 2016)
