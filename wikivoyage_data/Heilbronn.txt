
Heilbronn is a city with 117,000 inhabitants in Baden-Württemberg in Germany.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
Stuttgart airport is the closest with international connection although Frankfurt airport has a greater number of connections.

By rail[edit]
Heilbronn has direct rail connections to Stuttgart, Heidelberg, Mannheim and Würzburg as well as S-Bahn to Bretten, Karlsruhe and Schwäbisch Hall.

By car[edit]
The A 6 from Mannheim to Nürnberg and the A 81 from Stuttgart to Würzburg meet near Heilbronn at Weinsberger Kreuz.
