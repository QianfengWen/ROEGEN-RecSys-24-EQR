
Fairbourne is a small village on the coast of Gwynedd west of Dolgellau.


Understand[edit]
South of the River Mawddach estuary.

Get in[edit]
On the A493 9 miles from Dolgellau.
Lloyds coache 28[dead link] from Tywyn and Dolgellau
52.696-4.0491 Fairbourne railway station. Cambrian Coast Railway with services from Pwllheli and Machynlleth. Train in each direction only every two hours.   (updated Feb 2024)

52.717722-4.0429511 Barmouth Bridge. 900 yards (820 m) maily wooded bridge with single rail track and foot crossing of the Afon (river) Mawddach to Barmouth   
Do[edit]
 The beach. Lovely long sandy beach. However access over boulders hard for people with poor mobility. (updated Feb 2024)

 Fairbourne golf club. Bar open to visitors. (updated Feb 2024)
 Mawddach Trail. Walk or cycle along the 9 mile stretch of former railway line, that runs along the scenic estuary of the Mawddach river, from Morfa Mawddach, through Penmaenpool towards Dolgellau.
