
Maysville, in Kentucky's Northern Ohio River Region, is an old historic town located on the banks of the Ohio River.


Get in[edit]
Amtrak serves Maysville via its thrice-weekly Cardinal train, running between Chicago, Indianapolis, Cincinnati and Washington, D.C., New York. Westbound trains arrive around midnight while eastbound ones arrive early morning around 5 AM. However, note that delays are quite common. 38.652156-83.771111 Maysville station is located at the intersection of West Front Street and Rosemary Clooney Street, west of downtown.
