
Heath is a city in Licking County in the state of Ohio.


Get in[edit]
Map of Heath (Ohio)
By Car[edit]
From Interstate 70, take the State Route 79 North exit 129B and go north 6 miles.
From State Route 16, take the State Route 79 South exit and go south 2 miles.

By Plane[edit]
40.024722-82.4619441 Newark-Heath Airport. A general aviation airport and home of the Air Force Metrology and Calibration Program Office.   (updated Dec 2018)
Get around[edit]
By Car[edit]
Heath has no mass transit, so a vehicle will be needed to travel in the city.

By bike[edit]
There are two bike trails in the city. The Buckeye Scenic Trail connects with Newark.

See[edit]
Great Circle Earthworks
