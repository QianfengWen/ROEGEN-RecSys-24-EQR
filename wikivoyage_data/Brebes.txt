
Brebes is a regency in Central Java.


Understand[edit]
Brebes is known for onion farming and the industry of salted-egg production.

Get in[edit]
Map of Brebes


By car[edit]
From Cirebon take to the east for 50 km, and after passing through the border between West Java and Central Java, you will be in Brebes.
From Tegal take to the west for 11 km.

By train[edit]

Eat[edit]
Brebes is also known for the dish made from blengong meat. Blengong is a cross between a common duck and a muscovy duck.

-6.906313109.0710621 Blengong Goreng Ibu Sairoh, Lembarawa, ☏ +62 822 272 56700. 11:00-16:00. Serves deep fried blengong. Rp35,000. (updated Oct 2022)
-6.864688109.0479382 Kupat Blengong Kasturi Rajak, Jl. KH. Hasyim Ashari, Pasar Batang. W-Su, 14:30-21:00. Serves Brebes specialty dish kupat blengong, a rice cake in coconut milk soup with sate blengong (blengong meat skewers). (updated Oct 2022)
-6.926438108.8598133 Rujak Belut Ibu Ribut, Ciampel Kulon, Kersana, ☏ +62 877 308 48017. 08:00-20:00. Local Brebes cuisine, rujak belut, fried eels in a peanut sauce with slices of shallot, and fresh tomato. Rp30,000. (updated Oct 2022)
-7.259187109.0088134 Ketan Pencok Majapahit, Jl. Majapahit No.16, Pesantren, Kalierang, Bumiayu, ☏ +62 822 599 92213. 05:00-21:00. A special snack from Bumiayu District in Southern Brebes, ketan pencok, a sticky rice cake with pencok on top. Pencok is stir-fried grated coconut mixed with brown sugar. (updated Oct 2022)
