
Sagamore Hills is a township in Summit County in Northeast Ohio.


Get in[edit]
Map of Sagamore Hills
Sagamore Hills lies on the edge of the Cuyahoga Valley National Park.

By car[edit]
I271 loops around the eastern edge of Sagamore Hills.

By bike[edit]
 Cuyahoga Bike and Hike Trail. This paved bike trail connects Sagamore Hills with the Cuyahoga Valley National Park and the communities that border it including Hudson, and Stow. At the county line it connects to the Portage Trail connecting to Kent. (updated Feb 2019)
 Ohio and Erie Canal Towpath Trail. A compacted gravel path that goes north all the way to south Cleveland, near the Zoo. Follows the historic canal path. (updated Feb 2019)
