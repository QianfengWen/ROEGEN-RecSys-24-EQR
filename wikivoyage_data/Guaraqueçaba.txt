

Bay of Guaraqueçaba
Guaraqueçaba is a small town in Coastal Paraná.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
BR-277 from Curitiba via Paranaguá
PR-410 (Graciosa) from Curitiba
PR-405 from Antonina. This route is unpaved and can be in pretty bad condition during the summer months.
By bus[edit]
Viação Graciosa has a bus departing from Curitiba at 7AM. Tel. +55 41 3482-1236.
By boat[edit]
Ferries for Guaraqueçaba leave the dock in Paranaguá at 9AM and 1PM, with return trips at 6AM and 2PM. Tel. +55 41 3425-4542.

Get around[edit]
See[edit]
 Ararapira. Now a ghost town, this village was founded by the Portuguese crown in the mid-18th century and abandoned in the 1940s. Most of the houses have been eaten away by the waves, and only the cemetery is still in use today. 
 Igreja do Nosso Senhor Bom Jesus dos Perdões. 
 Superagüi National Park (Parque Nacional do Superagüi). Largely untouched mangue ecosystem, including the Ilha do Superagüi. Boat tours available. 
 [dead link] Salto Morato Nature Reserve (Reserva Natural Salto Morato), access from PR-405 (4km kilometers from the turnoff), ☏ +55 41 3381-9671. Tu-Su 8:30AM-5:30PM. Has a visitor center with information about tropical forest and Atlantic Forest ecosystems. R$7. 
 Tagaçaba River (Rio Tagaçaba). 
Do[edit]
 Morro do Quitumbê (Quitumbê Hill). This short trail starts beside the Igreja do Nosso Senhor Bom Jesus dos Perdões, leading to an exceptional spot for watching the sunset.
