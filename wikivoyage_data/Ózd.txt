
Ózd is an industrial town in Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén County in Northern Hungary, 53 km (33 mi) from the county seat of Miskolc.
Nearby town Borsodnádasd is also described here.


Understand[edit]
48°13′12″N 20°17′29″EMap of Ózd

The area has been inhabited since ancient times. The village of Ózd was first mentioned in 1272. The modern town came into being with the unification of the villages Ózd, Bolyok and Sajóvárkony during the socialist era of Hungary, when the northern part of the country was developed into a centre of heavy industry. Ózd is one of the poorest towns in Hungary.
