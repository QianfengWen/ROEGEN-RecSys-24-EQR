
Mims is a city in Brevard County.

By car[edit]
The main route to Mims is US-1, which travels south from New Smyrna Beach directly through Edgewater and Oak Hill to Mims. It continues south to Titusville. I-95 parallels this route roughly a mile to a few miles west of US-1. FL-46 enters Mims from the west.
