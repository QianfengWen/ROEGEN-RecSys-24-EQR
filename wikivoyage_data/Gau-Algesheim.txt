


Gau-Algesheim is in small town in Rhenish Hesse.


Understand[edit]
The small villages of Laurenziberg and Ockenheim, also belongs to the community.

Get in[edit]
On the B41 west of Ingelheim just off the A60 (east of A61 interchange).
The train station has connections to  Frankfurt–Koblenz (linke Rheinstrecke) and Mainz–Saarbrücken (via Bad Kreuznach).

Do[edit]
There are a number of short hiking trails in the area. Laurenziberg, is on the Rheinhessischer Jakobsweg (pilgrim way).

Buy[edit]
There are a number of "Weinguter" in and around the village where you can purchase local wines.
