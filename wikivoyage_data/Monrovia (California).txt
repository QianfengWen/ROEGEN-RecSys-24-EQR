
Monrovia is a city in Los Angeles County in Southern California.


Get in[edit]
Map of Monrovia (California)

Take the Myrtle Avenue off ramp from the 210 freeway and go north. Old Town is less than a mile from the freeway. The Metro Gold Line light rail service to Pasadena and Downtown L.A. stops in Monrovia with a stop at Myrtle Avenue and Duarte Road.

Get around[edit]
Once you get into Old Town you will see that parking is free, and much of the town can be seen on foot. There is a free trolley that runs during the day, that will port you from Old Town to other shopping areas.
