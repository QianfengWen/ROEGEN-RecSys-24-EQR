
Aufseß a municipality of 1,300 people (2019) in Upper Franconia.  It is most well known as the village with the most beer breweries per capita, however it can be thought of as a set of hamlets along the St2188, each with their own brewery.
The community of Aufseß has been listed in September 2000 in the Guinness Book of Records as world record-holder for
the greatest brewery density in the world.
