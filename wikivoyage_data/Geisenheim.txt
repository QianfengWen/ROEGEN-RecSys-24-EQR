
Geisenheim is a town in the Rheingau of Hesse, Germany. It includes the villages (Stadtteil) of Johannisberg and Marienthal.

Geisenheim Dom

Understand[edit]
The home of Spätlese ("late harvest") wine. The Geisenheim Grape Breeding Institute was founded here in 1872, the only institution in Germany where you can get a degree in Winemakeing.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Is on the B42 between Oestrich-Winkel and Rüdesheim.

By train[edit]
Train station on the line: Frankfurt – Wiesbaden – Rüdesheim – Koblenz – Neuwied

On foot[edit]
Johannisberg and Marienthal are on the Rheinsteig.
