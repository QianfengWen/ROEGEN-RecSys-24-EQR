
Beaudesert is a town in the is in the South East Queensland region of Queensland in Australia.
Beaudesert is the main centre of the Scenic Rim Local Government Area. 


Understand[edit]
Beaudesert takes its name from a pastoral station, established in 1842 by an early resident.
