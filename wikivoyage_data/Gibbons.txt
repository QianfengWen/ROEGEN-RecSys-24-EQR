
Gibbons (pop. 3,200 in 2016) in the Edmonton Capital Region, located north of the city of Edmonton. Its close neighbour is Bon Accord (pop. 1,500), an even smaller town located just to the west.


Understand[edit]
Map of Gibbons
Gibbons and Bon Accord are neighbouring agricultural and bedroom communities located in Sturgeon County that are about 7 km (4.3 mi) apart via Highway 28.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Gibbons and Bon Accord are about 20 minutes north of Anthony Henday Drive (Highway 216), the general northern extent of the built up area of North Edmonton and can accessed via Highway 28 (97 Street in Edmonton).  Gibbons can be accessed more directly by Highway 28A, which connects to Manning Drive (Highway 15) in Edmonton.

By plane[edit]
The Edmonton International Airport YEG IATA is about 45 minutes away.

Get around[edit]
You will need a private car as there is no public transit.
