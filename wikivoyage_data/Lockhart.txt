
Lockhart is in the Riverina region of New South Wales, Australia with a population of 818 (2016).


Understand[edit]
Lockhart is called Verandah Town because of the large number of intact shop verandahs along Green Street.

Get in[edit]
The journey is approximately 42 mins with no traffic from Wagga. There's no buses or trains that run here, so your only way to get here is by car. From Wagga, just head west onto A20 until Collingullie. From Collingullie, head south west onto Lockhart road until Lockhart. The road is pretty straightforward, with little winding curves.
