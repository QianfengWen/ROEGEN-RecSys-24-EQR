
Consolacion is in the Philippines; it is part of Metro Cebu and located just north of Mandaue. It is home to about 148,000 people (census, 1 May 2020) .


Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
The Central Nautical Highway runs through the center of the urban area of Consolacion.

By bus[edit]
Bus and jeepney route 25 between North Terminal Mandaue and Liloan (Metro Cebu).

Go next[edit]
Anywhere on Cebu Island is readily accessible by road; in particular Cebu City is nearby and Moalboal is popular with divers. Since Consolacion is north of Cebu City, one might also head further north to Danao and get a ferry to the Camotes Islands.
