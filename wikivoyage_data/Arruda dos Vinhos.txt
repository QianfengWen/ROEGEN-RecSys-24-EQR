
Arruda dos Vinhos is a town in Portugal's Oeste region.


Understand[edit]
Aqueduct in Arruda dos Vinhos.

Arruda dos Vinhos (/ɐˈʁuðɐ ðuʒ ˈviɲuʃ/, uh-RROO-thuh thoozh VEE-nyoosh) has 13,391 residents (2011).

Get in[edit]
The A10 motorway serves Arruda.
Boa Viagem offers several bus routes into Arruda, including route 88 from Lisbon.
The nearest train service stops at Alhandra, Alverca and Vila Franca de Xira.
