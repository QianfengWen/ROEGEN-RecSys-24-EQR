
New Norfolk is on the eastern edge of the Derwent Valley in Southern Tasmania. 


Get in[edit]
Map of New Norfolk
New Norfolk is just a half an hour drive from Hobart CBD via A10 or B10, you can choose your route depending on which side of the river you want to go through. There is also trains to Derwent Valley Railway New Norfolk Station, but the ride may not be as enjoyable as going in your own car.
