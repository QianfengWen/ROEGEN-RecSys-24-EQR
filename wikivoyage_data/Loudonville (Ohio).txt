

Loudonville
Loudonville is a village in Northwest Ohio, in the southern part of Ashland County, close to the Mohican State Park and Forest. It is a popular recreational area for outdoor activities.

Get around[edit]
A paved bike path runs from downtown Loudonville South of town all the way to the Mohican State Park. The paved bike path connects into the mountain bike trail system that runs through the Mohican State Park.
