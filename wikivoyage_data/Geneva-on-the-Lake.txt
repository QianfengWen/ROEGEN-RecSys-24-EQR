
Geneva-on-the-Lake is a village in Ashtabula County, Ohio.


Understand[edit]
Geneva-on-the-Lake is a small touristy village on the banks of Lake Erie known for being a summer vacation spot for northeast Ohio residents.

Visitor Information[edit]
