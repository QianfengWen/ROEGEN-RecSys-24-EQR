
Thorold is a city in Ontario, Canada, near Niagara Falls. It is known for the Welland Canal.


Understand[edit]
Maplehurst
Thorold is known mainly for the Welland Canal. In 2016, its population was 18,800.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Provincial Highways 406 and 20 run through it.

By bus[edit]
Niagara Region Transit. Route 50 and Route 55 from St. Catharines and Niagara Falls.
By plane[edit]
The closest major airports are Niagara Falls/Niagara South Airport, Niagara District Airport, and Buffalo Niagara International Airport.
