
Mirandela is a city in Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro, Northern Portugal.


Understand[edit]
Pedestrian bridge over the river.

The city of Mirandela (/miɾɐ̃ˈdɛlɐ/, meer-uhng-DEH-luh) has a population of about 15,000, and the municipality has 23,850 residents (2011).

Get in[edit]
The main motorway is the A4 (Autoestrada Transmontana or Autoestrada de Trás-os-Montes e Alto Douro), which also carries the designation IP4 and the European route number E82. Porto is less than 2 hrs away. A4 also connects from Bragança and Zamora (Spain).

41.4814-7.18111 Central de Camionagem (Bus Station), R. Dom Afonso III 278. Bus service is provided by Rodonorte, Grupo Santos, Rede Expressos, and Autoviação do Tâmega (AVT). (updated Jun 2021)
