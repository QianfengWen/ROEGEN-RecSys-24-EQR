
Rengsdorf is a small town on the edge of the Westerwald above the Middle Rhine Valley.


Understand[edit]
The home of Friedrich („Fritz“) Karl Henkel, founder of the Henkel company (investors of Persil).

Get in[edit]
On the  B256 between Neuwied and the A3 Altenkirchen exit.
The closest rail station is Neuwied on the right hand Rhine line. The Köln–Frankfurt high-speed rail line runs near the town, but the closest station on this line is Montabaur, about three-quarters of an hour by car.
