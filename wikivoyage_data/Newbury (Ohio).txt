
Newbury Township is in Geauga County in Ohio. It is about an hour outside Cleveland.


Get in[edit]
Map of Newbury (Ohio)
Drive East from Cleveland. Take I77 S to I480 E to US 422 E and head north on OH306 then go east on OH 87.
Drive North from Akron. Take I77 N to I271 N to US 422 E and head north on OH306 then go east on OH 87.

Get around[edit]
See[edit]
 South Newbury Union Chapel. Historic site where Susan B. Anthony spoke. 
 American Society of Metals, Russell. Has a large geodesic dome, visitor center, and rock garden. 
 West Woods, Russell. A Township Park with an interactive visitor's center and nature center. Take the trail to Ansel's Cave and see the many waterfalls.
