
Tettnang[dead link] is a town of 20,000 people (2020) in the Lake Constance (Bodensee) region of Baden-Württemberg about 10 km from Friedrichshafen. The region produces a special variety of hops, an ingredient of beer, and ships it to breweries throughout the world.

By plane[edit]
The small international airport in Friedrichshafen, Bodensee-Airport Friedrichshafen (FDH IATA), is only 6km away.

By car[edit]
10 km (6 miles) north-east of Friedrichshafen, 10 km south of Ravensburg.

By bus[edit]
Public transport is organised by Bodensee-Oberschwaben Verkehrsverbund (also called bodo).

Do[edit]
 Tettnang Hop Trail. A 4-km (or about 8-km round trip) hiking or cycling trail with information signs along the way about hops. Start- and endpoints are the Kronen-Brauerei (in the town centre) and Hop Museum. See online map. (updated Jan 2016)
 Tettnang Hops Circuit. A 42-km round trip cycling tour through Tettnang and its region (including Friedrichshafen and parts along Lake Constance). The route has a total ascent of about 160 m. There a signs (black bicycle on green arrow) along the way. See online map. (updated Jan 2016)
