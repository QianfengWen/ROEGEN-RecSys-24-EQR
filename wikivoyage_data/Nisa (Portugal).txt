
Nisa is a town and municipality in Alto Alentejo, Portugal.


Understand[edit]
Nisa's parish church.

Nisa (NEE-zuh, /ˈni.zɐ/) municipality has 5,951 residents (2021 preliminary).

Get in[edit]
Rede Expressos offers long-distance intercity express bus service.
Rodoviária do Alentejo provides regional bus service.
The nearest motorway is the A23 (Autoestrada da Beira Interior) from Guarda, Covilhã, Castelo Branco, Abrantes, and Torres Novas.
