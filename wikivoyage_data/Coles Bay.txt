
Coles Bay is on the Eastern part of Tasmania.


Understand[edit]
The town is the main service centre for Freycinet National Park.  The population is about 470 with lots of tourists particularly in the summer months.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Coles Bay is 28km from the A3 Tasman Highway.
