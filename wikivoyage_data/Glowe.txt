
Glowe is a small seaside resort on the island of Rügen.


Understand[edit]
Modernised village with good beach. 

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Is on the L30 between Sagard and Breege-Juliusruh.

Eat[edit]
There are a number of restaurants along main road. At the north end of the village are a number of fast food stall, for example the Curry Lounge for classic German Imbis food.
Also check out the fish stall in the Netto supermarket car park. Great fish rolls (Bismarckhering Brötchen).
