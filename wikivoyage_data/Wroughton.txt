
Wroughton is a large village in the county of Wiltshire. It is part of the Borough of Swindon.


Understand[edit]
Wroughton is one of the biggest parishes in the UK and boasts a population of more than 7,000.

Get in[edit]
It is about 4 miles (6.4 km) south of Swindon town centre, the town of  Marlborough  is about 10 miles (16.1 km) south.
