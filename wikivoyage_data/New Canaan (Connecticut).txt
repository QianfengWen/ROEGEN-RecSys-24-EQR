
New Canaan is a town in Connecticut. The town is one of the most affluent communities in the United States. In 2008, CNN Money ranked New Canaan first in the nation with the highest median family income.

Get in[edit]
By train[edit]
New Canaan has two Metro-North railroad stations: 41.1463-73.49561 New Canaan station and 41.116-73.49812 Talmadge Hill station, both on the New Canaan Branch of the New Haven Line. 
Travel time to Grand Central Terminal in New York City is approximately one hour.
