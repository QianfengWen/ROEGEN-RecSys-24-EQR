
Lucknow is in the Central West region of New South Wales, Australia, a couple of kilometres east of Orange.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Lucknow is a couple of minutes east of Orange, and about 2½ hours west of Sydney via the A32 and about 30-40 mins west of Bathurst again via the A32. Well, to come to Lucknow from Sydney, you have to pass the A32 in Bathurst; you've got no choice.

By plane[edit]
Orange Regional Airport OAG IATA has flights from Sydney, Melbourne and Brisbane, and is about 9 km south of Lucknow. Just the maximum capacity to walk.
