
Leonardtown is a city in Southern Maryland.


Get in[edit]
Map of Leonardtown


By car[edit]
Leonardtown is on Route 5 about 1.5 hours from Annapolis and 1 hour from Washington D.C. The Southern Maryland express can take visitors between the area and major airports nearby.

See[edit]
Leonardtown has a vibrant arts and entertainment district, home to a music school, art galleries, a chocolatier, a painting studio, and shops for antiques and flowers.

Do[edit]
 Port of Leonardtown Public Park, ☏ +1 301 475-9791, fax: +1 301 475-5350, Leonardtown.Commissioners@verizon.net. A little known, secret hidden along the McIntosh Run in Leonardtown is the Port of Leonardtown Public Park. This developing passive park and winery is the launch point of a nearly 3-mile, serene canoe/kayak trail. As you paddle along the McIntosh Run you will pass through a 58-acre Forest Interior Dwelling Species Habitat. This area is home to bald eagle families, Baltimore Orioles, wild turkeys and many other bird species. The trail terminates at the Leonardtown Wharf Public Park. Bring a picnic lunch or stroll up to Leonardtown Square and enjoy lunch at a restaurant. 
Buy[edit]
The local Amish communities have auctions where they sell crafts like quilts.
