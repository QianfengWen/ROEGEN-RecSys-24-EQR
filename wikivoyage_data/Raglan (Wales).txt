

Raglan castle
Raglan (Welsh: Rhaglan) is a village in Monmouthshire.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
Half way between Monmouth and Abergavenny on the A40 road very near to the junction with the A449 road from Newport (Monmouthshire).
83 bus from Monmouth and Abergavenny.
