
Haverfordwest (Welsh: Hwlffordd) is the county town of Pembrokeshire in South Wales.


Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Haverfordwest is on the main A40 London to Fishguard trunk road.

By rail[edit]
51.803-4.961 Haverfordwest station is served by trains on the West Wales Line from Swansea to Milford Haven, operated by Arriva Trains Wales

Do[edit]
Football: Haverfordwest County play soccer in the Cymru Premier League. That's the top tier within Wales, but mostly amateur and on a par with England's fifth tier National League. They play at Bridge Meadow Stadium (capacity 2100) just north of town centre.
