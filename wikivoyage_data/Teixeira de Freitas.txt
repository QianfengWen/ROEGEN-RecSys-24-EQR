
Teixeira de Freitas is in Southern Bahia.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
BR-101 connects the city to Itamaraju, Eunápolis, and Itabuna in the north and to São Mateus, Linhares, and Vitória to the south in Espírito Santo. About 35 km south of Teixeira de Freitas, BR-101 crosses BR-418 from Teófilo Otoni in Minas Gerais to the west.
BA-696 connects with BR-418 out of Caravelas about 50 km to the east.
