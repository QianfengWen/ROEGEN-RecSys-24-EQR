
Welshpool (Welsh: Y Trallwng) is a town in Montgomeryshire, Mid Wales.


Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Welshpool is at the cross-roads of the A483 (north/south) and the A458 (east/west). The A458 connects to the UK Motorway network at the M54 near Shrewsbury

By train[edit]
52.657469-3.1399471 Welshpool station. On the Shrewsbury to Aberystwyth line.   
By plane[edit]
52.629444-3.15252 Mid Wales Airport (ICAO:EGCW or Welshpool Airport) (just to the south of the town). Light aircraft.   
By boat[edit]
The Montgomery Canal passes through the town. A few miles either side of the town are navigable, but restoration work continues on the rest of the canal.

See[edit]
52.658-3.1451 Powysland Museum (Y Lanfa), The Wharf, SY21 7AQ, ☏ +44 1938 553001, ylanfa@powys.gov.uk. M 10AM-12:30PM, Tu 2-4PM, F Sa 10AM-12:30PM. Local museum focusing on archaeology and social/agricultural history. It's housed in a warehouse by Montgomery Canal. Free. (updated Jul 2021)
52.6492-3.157642 Powis Castle and Garden, SY21 8RF (a short distance out of town on the Newtown road), ☏ +44 1938 551944, powiscastle@nationaltrust.org.uk. Medieval fortress turned grand manor house, now in the care of the National Trust. The castle has a large collection of important paintings and furniture, as well as numerous treasures from India in the Clive Museum. The extensive gardens look much as intended when they were laid out in the 1770s are an attraction in their own right.   
Do[edit]
52.645341-3.2502361 Welshpool and Llanfair Light Railway, Raven Square Station, SY21 7LT, ☏ +44 1938 810441, info@wllr.org.uk. This steam-hauled, narrow gauge railway links Welshpool with the village of Llanfair Caereinion, 8.5 miles away.   (updated Apr 2018)
The Offa's Dyke Path national walking trail passes through the town. This is a long distance walking trail following the length of the England/Wales border from Prestatyn to Chepstow
52.790508-3.0904652 Llanymynech Golf Club, Pant. Former US Master's Winner and Ryder Cup Captain Ian Woosnam learnt his game here. This course also holds the distinction that it is the only course in the world in two countries, it has 15 holes in Wales and 3 in Shropshire, England. 
Buy[edit]
52.65691-3.1411021 The Old Station, Severn Road, SY21 7AY (Follow signs to the station from the town centre), ☏ +44 1938 556622, fax: +44 1938 554288. When the railway line was moved to accommodate a new bypass road in the early 1990s, the historic station building was left redundant. Now it has been turned into a mini department store, with gifts, clothes, woollens, golf equipment and a coffee shop and restaurant. 
52.639442-3.153072 Coed y Dinas, SY21 8SA (about a mile out of town on the Newtown road), ☏ +44 1938 555545. M-Sa 8:30AM-5:30PM, Su 10AM-4PM. Sprawling retail mecca in converted Victorian farm buildings. Garden Centre, equestrian store, pet shop, food hall, furniture outlet, kitchenware. Part of the same group as Cheap Charlies, whose shops in Aberystwyth and Newtown have been popular with local bargain hunters for years.

Connect[edit]
As of May 2022, there is 4G from all UK carriers in Welshpool and on A483 the north-south highway, but not much signal east or west. 5G has not reached this area.
