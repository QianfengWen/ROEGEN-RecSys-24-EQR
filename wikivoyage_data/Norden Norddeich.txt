Norddeich - View from the dyke
Norden-Norddeich is a nice old North-Sea seaside town in the Eastern Frisia region of Lower Saxony in Germany. It is visited by many people, who spend their vacation there.


Understand[edit]
The literal translation of Norden Norddeich is North Northerndike.

Get in[edit]
By train[edit]
Due to its importance as an access point for seaside resorts on the East Frisian Islands, 53.6244447.1588891 Norddeich-Mole.    is the last northbound stop for many long distance trains of Deutsche Bahn, especially during the summer season - it is very close to the ferry-dock, but awkward for access to the city. Almost all trains also stop at 53.6211117.1616672 Norddeich station.    and 53.58857.21893 Norden station.    which are more conveniently located to the town proper

By plane[edit]
53.6330567.1888894 Norden-Norddeich Airport (NOD  IATA). A special airfield accommodating general aviation as well as scheduled flights to the East Frisian Islands.
