
Crawfordville is a city on the Forgotten Coast of Florida.


Get in[edit]
Map of Crawfordville
The main road that goes to Crawfordville is US Route 319, which connects it to Sopchoppy and Tallahassee. Shadeville Road, also called Route 61, goes from nearby Shadeville to Crawfordville.

Get around[edit]
The Crawfordville Highway is the main road, and goes from the north of the town to the south.
