
Oliveira do Bairro is a city and municipality in Beira Litoral, Portugal.


Understand[edit]
The modernest Church of St. Peter (Igreja de São Pedro da Palhaça).

Oliveira do Bairro (aw-lee-VAY-ruh doo BIGH-rroo, /ɔ.ɫi.ˈvɐj.ɾɐ du ˈbɐi.ʁu/) municipality has 23,150 residents (2021 preliminary). The municipality includes the towns of Bustos, Mamarrosa, Oiã, Palhaça, and Troviscal.

Get in[edit]
40.5103-8.498911 Estação Ferroviária de Oliveira do Bairro (Oliveira do Bairro train station), Rua António Oliveira Rocha. PDF timetable. Oliveira is served by interregional and regional trains along the Linha do Norte (Northern Line) from Lisbon, Porto, Coimbra, and Aveiro. There is another station in the municipality at Oiã.  (updated Aug 2021)
Although the A1 motorway (Autoestrada do Norte) cuts through the municipality, the nearest exit (15) is north of the municipal boundary. From there, you can take the N235 road to reach the city.
The A17 motorway (Autoestrada do Litoral Centro) runs just outside the municipality's western edge and serves the towns there.
