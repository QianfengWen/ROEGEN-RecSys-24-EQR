
Hermannsburg is a country town in the district of Celle in Lower Saxony. It lies on the River Örtze in the Lüneburg Heath.


Get in[edit]
Hermannsburg can be reached by small roads, it is 10 km east of Bergen.
Accessible by bus from the nearest railway station Unterlüß: Cebus line 260 and Bürgerbus Südheide
from Bergen: Cebus line 210
from Faßberg / Müden: Cebus line 220
from Celle: Cebus line 200

Do[edit]
Explore the Southern Heath on the many footpaths and cycleways in the area that run through the Südheide Nature Park and its nature reserve the Naturschutzgebiet Heideflächen mittleres Lüßplateau
Canoe the Örtze by hiring a boat from one of the local canoe firms.
