Bellville is a village of 2,000 people (2020) in Richland County in the state of Ohio.

Get in[edit]
Map of Bellville (Ohio)
By car[edit]
Interstate 71, exit 165 at State Route 97 (Lexington-Bellville exit), go south on State Route 97, your right in Bellville. To get to the central business district, go south on State Route 97 to State Route 13.
Interstate 71, exit 169 at State Route 13 (Mansfield-Bellville exit), go south on State Route 13 to Bellville.
By bike[edit]
40.6229-82.51131 Richland B&O Trail. Goes north to Lexington and Mansfield, and south to Butler. (updated Feb 2019)
