
Câmara de Lobos is a city on the Portuguese island of Madeira.


Understand[edit]
Urban art made from recycled cans.

The city of Câmara de Lobos (KUH-muh-ruh d(ih) LOH-boosh, /ˈkɐ.mɐ.ɾɐ dɨ ˈɫo.buʃ/) has 17,986 residents (2011), while the entire municipality has 35,666. It's a suburb of Funchal, the island's capital. Its name translates as "Sea Lion Chamber".
