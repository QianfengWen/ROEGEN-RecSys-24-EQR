
Leinfelden-Echterdingen is in the Stuttgart Region of Germany.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By plane[edit]
Stuttgart airport is adjacent to the town.

By rail[edit]
Is on the S2 and S3 lines, Echterdingen is one station from Stuttgart airport while Leinfelden is one further on towards Vaihingen and Stuttgart centre.

By car[edit]
On the B27, 12 km from the centre of Stuttgart, just of the Stuttgart-Möhringen exit of the A8.
