
Satellite Beach is a city in Brevard County, Florida.


Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Two major causeways lead to the Satellite Beach area. One is the FL-404, or Pineda Causeway, which leads to South Patrick Shores north of Satellite Beach. Eau Gallie Boulevard leads from Eau Gallie (Melbourne) to Indian Harbour Beach south of Satellite Beach. A third causeway follows a barrier island parallel to Satellite Beach from Pineda Causeway to the southern side of Satellite Beach, where it connects to the southern end of the town.

Do[edit]
The primary attraction in Satellite Beach is the beach, which continues north and south of the city following the eastern shoreline. The Intracoastal Waterway on the west side of Satellite Beach is a popular destination for cruising on small craft.
