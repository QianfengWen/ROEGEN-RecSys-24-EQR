
Puycelsi is in the Tarn region of Occitanie, France.


Understand[edit]
Puycelsi is an ancient medieval village which was founded in the 10th century built on a hill.
It is a member of The Most Beautiful Villages of France, an association of rural villages.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Puycelsi can be reached by using the D 8A, D964 and D14 from Salvagnac.
