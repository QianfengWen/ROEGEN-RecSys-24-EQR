
Gresik is a regency in East Java. It's capital is Gresik City.


Understand[edit]
Aerial view of Gresik
Gresik has become an international trade center visited by many nations such as the Chinese, Arabs, Champa, and Gujarat since 11th-century. Gresik Regency is also believed as the first entry point for Islam in Java, which is marked by the existence of ancient Islamic tombs from Sheikh Maulana Malik Ibrahim or well known as Sunan Gresik.

By car[edit]
Gresik is 20 km northwest of Surabaya. It can be reached by car via the highway in more or less 35 minutes.

Events[edit]
Sanggring Kolak Ayam, The annual tradition in the month of Ramadan on the 23rd night is to cook a large amount of kolak ayam (Gresik chicken soup) for iftar in Gumeno village, Manyar district of Gresik. This tradition has been going on for more than 400 years.

Eat[edit]
Gresik cuisine[edit]
It's incomplete to visit Gresik without enjoying a portion of nasi krawu
Nasi krawu, rice with beef.
Sego Roomo, rice or rice cake topped with boiled spinach and crushed crackers, then layered with Roomo porridge (fine porridge seasoned with garlic, big chilies, galangal, and turmeric).
Bonggolan, an oval-shaped snack made from fish meat mixed with starch added with salt and garlic.
Pudak, made from rice flour, sugar, and coconut milk which are packed in a package called "ope", which is the midrib of areca leaves.
Kolak ayam, a local chicken soup dish.
Pepes telur ikan bader, steamed Java barb roe in banana leaves

Drink[edit]
