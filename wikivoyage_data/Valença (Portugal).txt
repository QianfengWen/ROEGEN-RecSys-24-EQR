
Valença, also known as Valença do Minho, is a city and municipality in Portugal's Minho region.


Understand[edit]
Corbels in Igreja de São Fins de Friestas.

Valença (vuh-LIHN-suh, /vɐ.ˈɫẽ.sɐ/) municipality has 13,634 residents (2021), with 5,153 living in the city proper. Valença lies on the River Minho across from Tui, Spain.

Get in[edit]
42.0248-8.63941 Estação Ferroviária de Valença (Valença Railway Station). PDF timetable. Trains serve Valença from Viana do Castelo, Porto, Coimbra, Figueira da Foz, and Vigo (Spain). Connections from Braga are available at Nine.   (updated Aug 2021)
42.0225-8.64262 Centro Coordenador de Transportes (Valença Bus Station), Avenida Sá Carneiro. Valença is accessible via various long distance and regional coach companies: Empresa de Transportes Courense, AVIC, Autna, Rede Expressos, and FlixBus. (updated Aug 2021)
Valença is served by the A3 motorway (Autoestrada de Entre-Douro-e-Minho) from Porto and Braga. Crossing the River Minho, it connects with Spain's A-55 expressway from Vigo.
