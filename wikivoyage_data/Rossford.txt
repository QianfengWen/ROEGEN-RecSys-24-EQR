
Rossford is a town in Northwest Ohio and suburb of Toledo known for it's shopping, casino, Marina, indoor sports facilities, bowling, parks and a vibrant historic district.

Built in 1928, this small airport had storied visitors such as Amelia Earhart, Jimmy Doolittle, and Charles Lindbergh before it was overshadowed by the more modern express airport.   (updated Jun 2020)
By car[edit]
Rossford is conveniently located on I-75 and on the 80/90 turnpike. It is a quick 15-minute drive south from Toledo a 2 hour drive north from Dayton and a 1 hour drive south from Detroit.

See[edit]

41.587849-83.5401731 Owens Community College (OCC), 30335 Oregon Rd, ☏ +1 567-661-7000. A small community college. The main campus was once a farm, and the land was gifted to the college on the condition that it's prominent silo remain standing. Aside from regular classes, it also offers some small workshops focused on personal enrichment, ranging from introductions to art and computer skills, to ghost hunting and beer appreciation. WiFi is available across campus.   (updated Nov 2018)
