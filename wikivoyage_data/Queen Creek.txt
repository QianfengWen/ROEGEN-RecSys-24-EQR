
Queen Creek is a town in far southeast of the East Valley the Greater Phoenix Metropolitan area.


Understand[edit]
Queen Creek is a town in the Greater Phoenix Metropolitan area, primarily in Maricopa County with the town limits extending into Pinal County.
Queen Creek is a growing town, from 26,000 (2010 census) to 59,519 (2020 census).

Get in[edit]
Queen Creek is in the far southeast of the East Valley of the Phoenix Metropolitan area.
It is located south of Mesa, south and east of Gilbert, east of Chandler Heights, and northwest of San Tan Valley.

By car[edit]
The only highway route in Queen Creek is Arizona State Route 24 (AZ 24), a connector highway to the Loop 202 (L-202) to the north; nearby is Arizona State Route 87 (AZ 87) to the west.
Major arterial streets include Ellsworth Rd, Hunt Hwy and Rittenhouse Rd.

By plane[edit]
Phoenix-Mesa Gateway Airport is the closest airport.
Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport is the closest major airport.
