
Greenwood is a city in the Nine-County Region of Indiana.


Get in[edit]
Map of Greenwood (Indiana)


By car[edit]
Greenwood sits just south of the capital, Indianapolis. I-65 heads north-south from Indianapolis into Greenwood. US-31 runs north-south through the city. US-37 runs north-south just to the west of the city.

By plane[edit]
 Indianapolis International Airport, 2500 S. High School Rd, Indianapolis, ☏ +1 317 487-7243. Greenwood is about 20 - 25 minutes from the airport. 
By bus[edit]
 Greyhound, 350 S. Illinois St, Indianapolis, ☏ +1 317 267-3076. Greenwood is about 25 minues from the bus station. Indianapolis would be the closest bus station. The next closest would be the one in Terre Haute, which is around 1 hour or so. 
By train[edit]
Amtrak 350 S. Illinois St., Indianapolis. Greenwood is about 25 minutes from the train station.

Festivals and events[edit]
Freedom Festival It offers a parade, the BBQ, food & business vendors, Old Towne Merchants Association Street Fair, live entertainment, concerts, kids' fairs and fireworks. It is held in late June/early July.
Farmer's Market Every Saturday morning, there is a local farmers' market.  There is great variety including fresh produce, honey, gourds, herbs, hanging baskets, cut flowers, and bedding plants.  It is found behind the Greenwood Public Library - south of Main Street off of Madison or Meridian Avenue.
Our Lady of Greenwood Festival It is held during the summer varying between June and July. It is a small festival with rides and games. Or, in a sense, it is like a small town carnival. It is located at Our Lady of Greenwood School.
