
Llangrannog is a village in Ceredigion, Wales.

View of the village

Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Take the B4334 or the B4321 off the A487 Cardigan - Aberystwyth road.

By bus[edit]
552 runs from Cardigan and Aberporth to Llangrannog and on to Aberaeron
