
Daajing Giids, formerly known as Queen Charlotte or Queen Charlotte City, and Skidegate (Haida: Hlg̱aagilda) are neighbouring villages on the southeast cost of Graham Island, Haida Gwaii, British Columbia.


Understand[edit]
Queen Charlotte harbour
Daajing Giids and Skidegate are neighbouring villages located 9 km (6 mi) apart. Daajing Giids offers several motels, shops, restaurants, a gas station and auto repair, a credit union, RCMP station and a hospital. With its small harbour, Daajing Giids is often the starting point for chartered tours into Gwaii Haanas National Park Reserve and Haida Heritage Site on southern Moresby Island. Skidegate Landing, the easternmost neighbourhood of Daajing Giids, includes the BC Ferries terminal serving the ferry route between Haida Gwaii and Prince Rupert (on mainland British Columbia), and the ferry route between Graham and Moresby Islands.
Prior to July 13, 2022, Daajing Giids was named Queen Charlotte.

By car[edit]
Daajing Giids and Skidegate are about an hour from Masset along the Yellowhead Highway (Highway 16).

By plane[edit]
The nearest airport is located in Sandspit (YZP IATA), which offers flights from Vancouver. Getting from Sandspit involves taking the Alliford Bay-Skidegate ferry.  The Masset Airport (ZMT IATA), over an hour away but still on Graham Island, offers flights from Prince Rupert.
