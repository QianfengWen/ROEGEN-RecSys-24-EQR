
Manorbier (Welsh: Maenorbŷr) is in Pembrokeshire.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
6 miles west of Tenby and 6 miles east of Pembroke on the B4139.

By train[edit]
51.66-4.7921 Manorbier station is about 1 mile north of the village. First Great Western[dead link] and Transport for Wales[dead link] operate here providing connections to Tenby, Pembroke Dock, Carmarthen and Swansea.

By bus[edit]
First Cymru, no.349 provides connections from Tenby, Haverfordwest and stops in-between such as Pembroke
