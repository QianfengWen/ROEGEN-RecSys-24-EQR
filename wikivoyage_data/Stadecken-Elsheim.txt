
Stadecken-Elsheim is reasonable sized village in Rhenish Hesse in the state Rhineland-Palatinate of Germany.


Understand[edit]
Set in the valley of the river Selz, surrounded by vineyards.

Get in[edit]
By car[edit]
Between Ingelheim and Nieder-Olm (A63 exit 4) on the L428/L416 or the L426 from Mainz and A60 exit 20.

By bus[edit]
ORN[dead link] buses available from Ingleheim, Nieder-Olm and Mainz.
