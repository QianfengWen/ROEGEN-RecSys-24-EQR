
Barmera is a town in the Murray and Mallee Riverland area in the east of South Australia.


Get in[edit]
34°12′58″S 140°19′59″EMap of Barmera
From Berri, it's within half an hours reach at max via the B201 and A20. There's no train line in Barmera, so your only way is by car.
