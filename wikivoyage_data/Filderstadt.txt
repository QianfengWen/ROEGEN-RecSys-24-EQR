
Filderstadt is in the Stuttgart Region of Germany.


Understand[edit]
It lies directly south of Stuttgart airport and Stuttgart Messe.
It consists of the communities of Bernhausen, Bonlanden, Harthausen, Plattenhardt and Sielmingen.
This article also includes the village of Wolfschlugen.
