
Mettlach is a municipality in the Merzig-Wadern district of the Saarland. It's at the western edge of Germany, bordering France, and 5 km east of the Schengen triple border of Germany, France and Luxembourg. Mettlach is best known for the manufacture of Villeroy & Boch ceramics.


Get in[edit]
Saarschleife
By train[edit]
Mettlach has two rail stations on the Saarstrecke that runs between Saarbrücken and Trier.

By car[edit]
Mettlach is on the B51 road to Konz, 9 km north of Merzig.
