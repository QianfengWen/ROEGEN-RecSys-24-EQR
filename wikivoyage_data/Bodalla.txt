
Bodalla is a town in the Eurobodalla region of southeastern New South Wales.


Understand[edit]
Get in[edit]
Bodalla is just on the A1 Princes Highway half an hour south of Batemans Bay and about 4-5 hours south of Sydney by car. There are no trains and buses are infrequent.

Eat[edit]
 Bodalla Dairy Shed, 52 Princes Highway. This local institution has grown from a humble shed into an almost mandatory pitstop for travellers. Cafe and retro milk bar with homemade ice cream and a range of cheeses. Calf feeding at 1 and 4pm. 
 Cafe Blue Earth, 160 Princes Highway, ☏ +61 4473 5211. Veggie friendly cafe getting good reviews.
