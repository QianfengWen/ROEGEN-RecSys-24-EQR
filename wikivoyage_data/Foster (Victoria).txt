
Foster is in the Gippsland region of Victoria, Australia.


Understand[edit]
Visitor information Centre[edit]
-38.650717146.1996211 Prom Country Visitor Information Centre - Foster, Cnr McDonald and Main Streets (C445), toll-free: 1800 630 704. 10AM-4PM. (updated May 2021)
Get in[edit]
From Melbourne, use the South Gippsland Freeway (M420) and exit onto the South Gippsland Highway (A420). Continue on the road for about 45 mins, until the A440 exit onto South Gippsland Highway. Continue on the highway until Foster, and turn right onto C445.
